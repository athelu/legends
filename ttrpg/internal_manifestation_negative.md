# Negative — Internal Manifestation

## Overview

The expression of Negative energy internally is entropy and decay directed through the self.  A character who channels Negative internally is using their own tissue as a conduit for a force that erodes tissue.

---

## Pairings

---

### Negative / Fire
**Category:** Expression
**Table:** Expression
**Tell:** Eyes darken at the edges. Targets struck feel an absence rather than an impact.
**Expression:** Strikes carry both fire damage and Negative energy damage, consuming the target's vitality as heat. The character can pay an HP cost to increase effect level. Presence activates: creatures within 20 feet make a Will save against the internal roll result or gain the Frightened condition (Level 1 Fear).
**Exhaustion:** Yes

| Primary Cost | Bonus Fire Damage | Bonus Negative Damage | Condition on Margin 2+ | HP Cost Option | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|------------------|----------------------|------------------------|----------------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +1 fire | +1 Negative | Weakened | 4 HP: performs as 5 cost | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | +2 fire | +2 Negative | Weakened + target DR -2 | 4 HP: performs as 7 cost | 1 — 1 round |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 fire | +4 Negative | Weakened + target DR -3 | 4 HP: Presence activates | 2 — 1 minute |

### Negative / Positive
**Category:** Unstable — GM adjudicates
**Table:** None
**Tell:** A quality of wrongness that observers find deeply disturbing regardless of outcome.
**Expression:** The two most fundamentally opposed energies channeled simultaneously through the body.
**Exhaustion:** Yes

**Balanced investment** (within 1 point of each other): The energies destabilize. The character makes a Fortitude save at difficulty 2 or gains one Exhaustion level immediately. Effect is unpredictable — GM determines outcome, which may be beneficial, harmful, or both.

**Negative dominates** (Negative investment significantly exceeds Positive): Amplified Negative effect — Negative/Earth or Negative/Air effect at one tier above normal investment. The Fortitude save still applies.

**Positive dominates** (Positive investment significantly exceeds Negative): Character takes HP damage equal to the Negative investment spent. Gains a Positive effect determined by the GM — the Positive energy won but the Negative cost was paid in tissue.
