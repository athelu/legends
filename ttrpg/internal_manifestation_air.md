# Air — Internal Manifestation

## Overview

Manifesting Air energy internally alows control of movement, pressure, vibration, and heightened sensitivity. Air energy enhances reaction, perception, and precision.

---

## Pairings

---

### Air / Fire
**Category:** Enhancement
**Table:** Buff/Debuff
**Tell:** The character must be in constant motion with subtle movements even while standing still. Their eyes dart and track faster than normal. The characters hair may even move in a sourceless personal wind.
**Expression:** Air energy supercharged by Fire energy produces explosive reaction speed. This is the body's ability to respond faster than normal. Heightened combat awareness, faster initiative, and reactions that border on precognition.
**Exhaustion:** no

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 to Initiative, subtract 1 from one Reflex save die this scene | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 to Initiative, subtract 1 from both Reflex save dice | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 Initiative, Fortune on Reflex saves, one additional Reaction per round | 3 — 10 minutes |


### Air / Earth
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff
**Tell:** Able to find purchase on impossible surfaces and no stumbling on difficult terrain.
**Expression:** Air energy compressed and grounded through Earth energy makes the character difficult to move, knock down, or destabilize. This is dynamic stability, offering the ability to flow and still not fall.
**Exhaustion:** no

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Immunity to Prone from physical sources (shoves, trips, knockdowns) | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | As above + cannot be moved involuntarily (immune to Shove, forced movement) | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + -2 to both dice on all Fortitude saves | 3 — 10 minutes |


### Air / Water
**Category:** Adaptation
**Table:** Transformation
**Tell:** The character's breath does not mist in cold. Their Skin stays dry regardless of humidity.
**Expression:** Air energy and Water energy combine internally to produce resistance to cold and enchanced environmental sensitivity. The character's body adjusts to temperature and pressure extremes as well as sensing pressure changes in air and water, detecting movement, approaching weather, and hidden creatures by air disturbance.
**Exhaustion:** no

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Ignore environmental cold penalties, blindsense within 15 feet | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Ignore cold conditions up to Frosted (Level 3),blindsense 30 feet | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | Immune to all cold conditions, blindsense 60 feet | 4 — 1 hour |

**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff
**Tell:** Slow, deep breathes with long exhalation. 
**Expression:** Air energy with Positive energy is a purified, resotrative breath that cleanses mental conditions, clears confusion, and restores sensory clarity.
**Exhaustion:** no

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|

| 7 (6+1) | Remove all current mental [Hindered] conditions, immunity to new mental conditions for duration | 3 — 10 minutes |


### Air / Negative
**Category:** Presence
**Table:** Presence
**Tell:** Air near the character feels thin and torches and flames within range dim and waver.
**Expression:** Air and Negative energy combined creates a suffocating entropy. The character's body becomes a source of life-draining absence. Nearby creatures feel vitality being pulled toward the character.
**Exhaustion:** yes

| Primary Cost | Range | Effect on Failed Save | Secondary Duration Cost |

| 7 (6+1) | 20 feet | Exhausted (2 Exhaustion levels) + Weakened — Fortitudesave each round | 3 — 10 minutes |
