## Conditions
There are a number of conditions that can be applied to a character. These often are the result of a spell weave or creature ability. Some Conditions scale, becoming more powerful as they stack up. A character must recover completely from the condition, often requiring more than one save. 

### Condition Keywords
 
Every condition carries one or both of the following keywords:
 
**[Hindered]** — The condition penalizes the affected creature's actions, movement, saves, or dice results. Feats and abilities that reference "a [Hindered] condition" apply to any condition carrying this keyword.
 
**[Tactical]** — The condition affects targeting, visibility, or positional calculations without directly penalizing the creature's own rolls. These are situational states that change how the creature interacts with the battlefield.
 
A condition may carry both keywords when it both penalizes the creature and creates tactical complications.
 

### Asleep
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Cannot take actions or reactions. Cannot move. Blinded condition active. Prone condition active. Automatically fail Fortitude and Reflex saves. Attacks against you have Fortune
**Recovery:** Loud noises or physical contact: Make Perception check (1 success to wake)

### Bleeding
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Take 2 damage per stack at the end of each of your turns. Multiple bleeding effects add together (2 stacks = 4 damage per turn, 3 stacks = 6 damage per turn). If at 0 HP or below, bleeding damage stops. Add your total bleeding stack count to both dice when making stabilization saves.
**Recovery:** Magical healing removes all bleeding stacks. Target may Use 1 action to make a Medicine check to reduce bleeding.
- 2 successes: Remove 1 bleeding stack
**Ally Assistance:** An ally within 5 feet may Use 1 action to make a Medicine check.
- 1 successes: Remove 1 bleeding stack
- 2 successes: Remove all bleeding stacks

### Blinded
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** The target loses their sight. Vision-based Perception checks automatically fail. Initiative -4. Attacks against you have Fortune. Your opponents have the hidden condition against your attacks.
**Recovery:** The source of the condition will describe the recovery conditions.

### Bound
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Tied up or bound in chains or shackles. Movement speed reduced to 0. Cannot take [Move] actions. All attacks and skill checks: Add 2 to both die results. Attacks against you have Fortune.Automatically fail Reflex saves.
**Recovery:** Use 1 action to make a Acrobatics, Might or devices check
- 2 successes: Remove Bound condition

### Charmed
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** The charmer has Fortune on Charisma-based checks against you. You cannot attack the charmer or target them with harmful abilities/weaves. You can still act freely otherwise and defend yourself. You interpret the charmer's words and actions in the most favorable way possible. You won't follow commands that are obviously harmful to yourself or that go against your core values. The charmer must still convince you through normal social interaction
**Recovery:** Condition ends immediately if charmer or their allies harm you. Some charm effects allow for a will save to end early.

### Clumsy
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Physical coordination is impaired. Dexterity and Agility based skill checks: Add 1 to ability die results. Reflex saves: Add 1 to die results.
**Recovery:** The source of the condition will describe the recovery conditions.

## Cold Effects
Cold is a stacking condition and making a successful saving throw will reduce the level of the condition down the stack. 

### Chilled (Level 1 Cold)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Cold has numbed extremities and slowed reactions slightly. Agility based skill checks: Subtract 1 from one die on your next action.
**Recovery:** Automatically ends after character’s next action.

### Numbed (Level 2 Cold)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Extremities significantly numbed, fine motor control impaired. Dexterity based skill checks: Add 1 to one die. Attacks with weapons: Add 1 to attribute die.
**Recovery:** Automatically ends after 1 minute. Fortitude save to end early.
- 1+ successes: Condition ends

### Frosted (Level 3 Cold)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Ice has formed on skin and clothing, movement impaired. Movement speed: Halved. Clumsy condition active
**Recovery:** Automatically ends after 1 minute. Can attempt Fortitude save at end of each turn to end early.
- 1 success: Reduce condition to Numbed
- 2 success: Remove all cold conditions

### Frozen (Level 4 Cold)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Body encased in ice or frozen solid. Restrained condition active Take 4 cold damage at the beginning of your turn.
**Recovery:**  Use 1 [Action] to make Might check.
- 1 success: subtract 1 from both dice on next attempt
- 2 success: Remove Frozen and Restrained condition.
**Ally assistance:** Ally within 5 feet Use 1 [Action] to make Might check to break target free.
- 1 success: subtract 1 from both dice on next attempt
- 2 success: Remove Frozen and Restrained condition.

### Concealed
**Keyword:** [Tactical]
**Effect:** Your location is known, but you are difficult to see clearly. Attackers add 1 to both dice when attacking you.
**Recovery:** Lasts as long as environmental condition persists.

### Dazed
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Mental processing slowed Next action taken: Add 1 to both dice. Moved to the bottom of the initiative order. Cannot take reactions until after your next turn.
**Recovery:** Automatically ends after your next turn completes

### Dazzled
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Target’s vision is impaired by bright light, spots, or over-stimulation. Vision-based Perception checks: Add 1 to both die results. Attack rolls: Add 1 to both die results.
**

### Dead
**Effect:** Character is deceased. Cannot be affected by normal healing.
**Recovery:** Requires resurrection magic or special circumstances

### Deafened
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** The target loses the ability to hear. Sound-based Perception checks automatically fail. Initiative: -2. Cannot benefit from verbal communication or warnings
**Recovery:** The source of the condition will describe the recovery conditions.

### Disoriented
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Spatial awareness or balance impaired. Perception checks: Add 1 to both die results. Initiative: -1. Opponents receive the concealed condition to your attacks.
**Recovery:** Typically 1 round unless specified otherwise.

### Dying
**Effect:** You are at 0 HP or below. Cannot take actions, reactions, or move. Unconscious condition active. Blinded condition active. Prone condition active
**Recovery:** See Dying Mechanic section

## Exhaustion Effects
Exhaustion is a stacking condition and making a successful saving throw will reduce the level of the condition down the stack. 
Exhaustion can be gained from the following:
- Marching for 8+ hours without rest: Fortitude save or gain 1 level
- Going without sleep for 24 hours: Automatic 1 level
- Extreme environmental conditions (heat, cold): Fortitude save per hour or gain 1 level
- Certain spells, poisons, or abilities

### Fatigued (Exhaustion Level 1)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** All skill checks: Add 1 to attribute die results.Cannot benefit from HP recovery for short rests.
**Recovery:** Long rest removes 1 level of Exhaustion

### Exhausted  (Exhaustion Level 2)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** All skill checks Add 2 to attribute die results. Movement speed reduced by half. Receive no benefit from short rests.
**Recovery:** Long rest removes 1 level of Exhaustion

### Severely Exhausted  (Exhaustion Level 3)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** All rolls add 2 to both die results. Movement speed reduced by half. Can only take 2 actions per turn instead of 3. Receive no benefit from short rests.
**Recovery:** Long rest removes 1 level of Exhaustion

### Near Collapse  (Exhaustion Level 4)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** All rolls add 3 to both die results. Movement speed reduced to quarter. Can only take 1 action per turn.Cannot take reactions.Receive no benefit from short rests.
**Recovery:** Long rest removes 1 level of Exhaustion

### Collapse  (Exhaustion Level 5)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Character becomes Unconscious. Cannot be awakened until Exhaustion reduced below level 5
**Recovery:** Long rest removes 1 level of Exhaustion

## Fear Effects
Fear is a stacking condition and making a successful saving throw will reduce the level of the condition down the stack. 
If the character can no longer see or perceive the fear source, they Subtract 1 from both of their recovery save die results.
If the source of fear is destroyed or defeated, the character immediately downgrades their fear stack one level.

### Frightened (Level 1 Fear)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Can act normally but suffers the penaltyAdd 1 to die results while the source of fear is perceived.
**Recovery:** At the end of each turn, make Will save.
- 1+ success: Condition ends
**Ally Assistance:** An ally within 5 feet can use an [Interact] action with Persuasion or Intimidate check:
- 1+ successes: Condition ends

### Fleeing (Level 2 Fear)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Must use movement to get away from the fear source by the safest1 route available. Cannot willingly move closer to the fear source. Can engage other threats that aren't the source of fear. Add 1 to all die results.
**Recovery:** At the end of each turn, make Will save.
- 1 success: Downgrade to Frightened
- 2 successes: Condition ends completely
**Ally Assistance:** An ally within 5 feet can use an [Interact] action with Persuasion or Intimidate check
- 1 success: Target may reroll next recovery save
- 2 successes: Downgrade to Frightened

### Cowering (Level 3 Fear)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Cannot take [Combat] actions. Cannot take [Move] actions. Attacks against you have Fortune.
**Recovery:** At the end of each turn, make Will save.
- 1 success: Downgrade to Fleeing
- 2 successes: Downgrade to Frightened
**Ally Assistance:** An ally within 5 feet can use an [Interact] action with Persuasion or Intimidate check.
- 1 success: Downgrade to Fleeing
- 2 successes: Downgrade to Frightened

## Fire Effects
Fire is a stacking condition and making a successful saving throw will reduce the level of the condition down the stack. 

### Singed (Level 1 Fire)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Hair or clothing lightly burned, minor distraction.Subtract 1 from one die on your next action.
**Recovery:** Automatically ends after your next action

### Smoldering (Level 2 Fire)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Take 2 fire damage at the beginning of your next turn only.
**Recovery:** Automatically ends after taking the 2 damage
**Ally assistance:** Ally within 5 feet can use 1 [Action]: Automatic extinguish

### Ignited (Level 3 Fire)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Character is on fire. Take 4 fire damage at the beginning of each of your turns.
**Recovery:** Use 1 [Action] to drop and roll: Make Reflex save
- 1 success: Reduce condition to Smoldering
- 2 success: Remove all fire conditions
**Ally assistance:** Ally within 5 feet can use 1 [Action]: Automatic extinguish

### Burning (Level 4 Fire)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Character is engulfed in intense flames. Take 8 fire damage at the beginning of each of your turns. All actions: Add 1 to die results.
**Recovery:** Use 1 [Action] to drop and roll: Make Reflex save
- 1 success: Reduce condition to Ignited
- 2 successes: Remove all fire conditions
**Ally assistance:** Ally can use 1 [Action]: Make Reflex save
- 1 success: Reduce condition to Ignited
- 2 successes:  Remove all fire conditions

### Grappled
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Movement speed reduced to 0. Cannot take [Move] actions. Attacks and skill checks: Add 1 to both die results.
**Recovery:** Use 1 action to make an opposed Athletics, Might or Acrobatics check.
- Margin 1+: Remove grappled condition.

### Grievously Wounded
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Applied when you regain consciousness at 0 HP or below. Can only take one action per turn. Cannot take reactions. Gain the Clumsy and Dazzled Conditions.
**Recovery:** Condition ends when healed above 1 HP

### Hidden
**Keyword:** [Tactical]
**Effect:** Your location is unknown; enemies do not know which square you occupy. Enemies cannot directly target you with attacks or single-target weaves. While hidden you have Fortune on your first [combat] action. Making a [combat] action removes the hidden condition. 
**Recovery:** Opposed by Observers perception check

### Invisibile
**Keyword:** [Tactical]
**Effect:** Magically induced state of Invisibility. Grants Hidden condition.
**Recovery:** Opposed by Observers perception check reducing effect to Concealed.

### Paralyzed
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Cannot take actions, reactions. Cannot move. Cannot speak. Automatically fail Fortitude and Reflex saves. Attacks against you have Fortune. May be aware of surroundings through senses.
**Recovery:** At the end of each turn, make Fortitude save.
- 1 success: Subtract 1 from both die results on next recovery save
- 2 successes: Remove Paralyzed condition

### Poisoned (Weak)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** All attribute dice: Add 1 to die results. HP regeneration from short/long rests reduced by half.
**Recovery:** Fortitude save at end of 1 hour (1 success). Medicine check (2 successes) can end Poisoned early.

### Poisoned (Strong)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** All attribute dice: Add 2 to die results.  HP regeneration from short/long rests reduced by half.Target takes 4 damage each hour until the condition is removed.
**Recovery:** Fortitude save at end of 4 hours (1 success). Medicine check (2 successes) can end Poisoned early.

### Poisoned (Deadly)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** All attribute dice: Add 2 to die results.  Long and short rests provide no benefit..Target takes 8 damage each hour until the condition is removed.
**Recovery:** Fortitude save at end of 4 hours (1 success). Antidote can remove condition early.

### Prone
**Keyword:** [Hindered], [Tactical]
**Effect:** You are lying on the ground. Melee attacks against you subtract 1 from both attack dice. Ranged attacks against you have Misfortune. Your melee attacks have Misfortune.
**Recovery:** Standing up from prone costs 1 [Move] action

### Restrained
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Placed in a hold or lock by an opponent. Movement speed reduced to 0. Cannot take [Move] actions. All attacks and skill checks: Add 2 to both die results. Attacks against you have Fortune.Automatically fail Reflex saves.
**Recovery:** Use 1 action to make an opposed Athletics, Might or Acrobatics check
- Margin 1: Reduce condition to Grappled.
- Margin 2: Remove Restrained condition.

### Revealed
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Magic or effects expose your true position and prevent concealment. You lose and cannot gain the Invisible, Hidden or Concealed condition.
**Recovery:** The source of the condition will describe the recovery conditions.

### Sickened
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Constitution and bodily functions impaired. Fortitude saves: Add 1 to die results. Cannot benefit from food or drink
**Recovery:** Typically 10 minutes to 1 hour unless specified otherwise. Medicine check (1 success) can end early

### Slowed (minor)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Movement speed reduced by 10 feet, Initiative -1.
**Recovery:** Unless otherwise specified, Will save at end of turn.
- 1 success: Remove Slowed condition

### Slowed (Major)
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Movement speed reduced by 10 feet, Can only take 2 action per turn. Initiative -1. Haste weave can counteract.
**Recovery:** Unless otherwise specified, Will save at end of turn.
- 1 success: Reduce condition to  Slowed (minor) 
- 1 success: Remove Slowed condition

### Stunned
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Cannot take reactions until after your next turn. Automatically fail Reflex saves. Attacks against you have Fortune.
**Recovery:** Fortitude save at the end of your turn (1 success)

### Surprised
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Cannot act during the surprise round. Add 2 to die results until your first turn.
**Recovery:** After surprise round ends, act normally

### Unconscious
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Cannot take actions or reactions. Cannot move. Blinded condition active. Prone condition active. Automatically fail Fortitude and Reflex saves. Attacks against you have Fortune.
**Recovery:** The source of the condition will describe the recovery conditions.

### Weakened
**Keyword:** [Hindered]
**Effect:** Physical strength impaired. Strength based skill checks: Add 1 to ability die results. Melee damage: Reduce by 2 (minimum 1). Carrying capacity: Halved
**Recovery:** Typically 1 minute to 1 hour unless specified otherwise
