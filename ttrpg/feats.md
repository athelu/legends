# D8 TTRPG - Part 5: Feats

## Feat System

### Prerequisites

#### Most feats require:
-   Minimum attribute (typically 4-5)
-   Minimum skill rank (typically 3-4)
-   Sometimes: Previous feat, advantage, or story requirement

### Combat Feats

#### Power Attack
-   Prerequisites: Str 4, Melee 3
-   Benefit: Declare before rolling: Add 1 to both die results, deal +1d8 damage on hit

#### Precise Shot
-   Prerequisites: Dex 4, Ranged 3
-   Benefit: Ignore penalties for shooting into melee/partial cover

#### Defensive Fighting
-   Prerequisites: Agi 4, Melee 3
-   Benefit: Defenseive Stance: opponents have Misfortune on attacks against you, you cannot attack while in defensive stance

#### Weapon Specialization
-   Prerequisites: Melee 4 or Ranged 4
-   Benefit: Choose one weapon type: Fortune when attacking with that weapon

#### Two-Weapon Fighting
-   Prerequisites: Agi 4, Dex 3
-   Benefit: When making dual-weapon attacks in a single [Attack] action, first attack is normal (no penalty), second attack adds 1 to both die results (instead of 2). You still use a Shared Attribute Roll for both attacks.

#### Shield Master
-   Prerequisites: Str 3, Melee 3, must be using a shield
-   Benefit: Reflex saves with shield: Subtract 1 from die results; once per combat as a [Reaction] completely negate one attack that would hit you (doesn't consume shield's normal reaction); you may treat your shield as a secondary weapon, gaining the benefits of Two-Weapon Fighting feat when using weapon + shield (shield deals 4 damage bludgeoning)

### Weaving Feats

#### Efficient Weaving
-   Prerequisites: Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, any Mastery 4
-   Benefit: One energy type: weaves cost 1 less Energy (min 1)

#### Weave Focus
-   Prerequisites: Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, any Mastery 3
-   Benefit: One energy: Subtract 1 from Potential and Mastery die results for casting rolls only

#### Metaweaving: Extend
-   Prerequisites: Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, Int or Wis 5
-   Benefit: Once per day: double weave duration, no extra cost

#### Metaweaving: Empower
-   Prerequisites: Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, any Mastery 5
-   Benefit: Once per day: Spend 2 extra Energy, roll damage twice, take
    higher per die

#### Ritual Weaver
-   Prerequisites: Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, Int or Wis 4
-   Benefit: 10-minute ritual: half Energy cost (min 1), not in combat

#### Wild Magic
-   Prerequisites: Magical Aptitude, Luck 5
-   Benefit: On double 1s/8s when weaving: roll on Wild Magic table for
    extra effect

#### Split Energy
-   Prerequisites: Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, two Masteries 5+
-   Benefit: Split weave to two targets/locations, each uses half Energy

#### Improved Channeling
-   Prerequisites: Divine Gift, Wis 5, Pos or Neg Mastery 4
-   Benefit: 40ft radius, 1d8 per 3 Energy, Turn/Command subtract 1 from one die result

#### Empowered Channeling
-   Prerequisites: Divine Gift, Pos or Neg Potential 6
-   Benefit: Once per day when channeling: treat the effect as if twice the Energy was spent, but only spend the normal amount

#### Selective Channeling
-   Prerequisites: Divine Gift, Wis 6, Pos or Neg Mastery 5
-   Benefit: Heal/Harm intent: exclude up to half Wisdom score (rounded down) targets

#### Potent Channeling
-   Prerequisites: Divine Gift, Pos or Neg Mastery 5
-   Benefit: Add Wisdom to healing/necrotic damage when channeling

#### Channel Focus
-   Prerequisites: Divine Gift, Pos or Neg Mastery 6
-   Benefit: One energy type: channel as bonus action (costs only 1 action instead of 2)

### Skill Feats

#### Skill Mastery
-   Prerequisites: Any skill rank 6
-   Benefit: Once per session, automatically succeed with 2 successes on a skill check (no roll required) if both your attribute and skill are 4 or higher

#### Jack of All Trades
-   Prerequisites: Int 4, five skills at 3+
-   Benefit: Rank 1 skills count as rank 2

#### Expertise
-   Prerequisites: Any skill rank 5
-   Benefit: For chosen skill: Fortune when using that skill

#### Linguist
-   Prerequisites: Int 4, Language 3
-   Benefit: Language rank: +2 for languages known, learn faster

#### Quick Learner
-   Prerequisites: Int 5
-   Benefit: All skill improvements cost 2 less XP

#### Medical Expert
-   Prerequisites: Wis 4, Medicine 4
-   Benefit: Fortune on Medicine checks, heal 1d8 additional HP

#### Master Craftsman
-   Prerequisites: Dex 5, Craft 5
-   Benefit: Work twice as fast, enhanced quality

#### Acrobatic Defense
-   Prerequisites: Agi 5, Acrobatics 4
-   Benefit: Unarmored: Subtract half Acrobatics rank from defense die results

### General Feats

#### Tough
-   Prerequisites: Con 4
-   Benefit: Gain HP equal to Con × current Tier

#### Fleet of Foot
-   Prerequisites: Agi 4
-   Benefit: 50% increase to movement, Dash action no penalty

#### Alert
-   Prerequisites: Wis 4, Perception 3
-   Benefit: Initiative: +2, cannot be surprised

#### Fortune's Favorite
-   Prerequisites: Luck 5
-   Benefit: Once per session, after seeing any roll result (yours or an enemy's), force a reroll. Choose which result to use.

#### Iron Will
-   Prerequisites: Wis 5
-   Benefit: Will saves: Subtract 1 from die results; once per session reroll failed Will save

#### Resilient
-   Prerequisites: Con 5
-   Benefit: Fortitude saves: Subtract 1 from die results; success on ongoing effect ends it

#### Charming
-   Prerequisites: Cha 5, Persuasion 4
-   Benefit: Once per session reroll failed social check, double 1s = ally

#### Danger Sense
-   Prerequisites: Wis 5, Luck 4
-   Benefit: GM gives check to sense danger, act on 2 successes

#### Ambidextrous
-   Prerequisites: Dex 4
-   Benefit: No off-hand penalties

#### Fast Healer
-   Prerequisites: Con 5
-   Benefit: Short rest: +Con HP, Long rest: double HP

#### Indomitable
-   Prerequisites: Con 6, Tier 4+
-   Benefit: Once per session: 0 HP → 1 HP + take one action

#### Evasion
-   Prerequisites: Agi 6, Tier 3+
-   Benefit: Reflex success = no damage, failure = half
