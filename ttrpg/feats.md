# D8 TTRPG - Part 5: Feats

## Feat System

### Prerequisites

#### Most feats require:
-   Minimum attribute (typically 4-5)
-   Minimum skill rank (typically 3-4)
-   Sometimes: Trait, Previous feat, or story requirement

### Combat Feats

#### Action Surge
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess, Con 5, any combat skill 5
- **Benefit:** Once per short rest, on your turn, gain 3 additional actions and reset your multiple action penalty to 0
- **Usage:** Once per short rest

#### Battle Master: Disarming Strike
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess, Str or Agi 5, chosen combat skill 5
- **Benefit:** When you hit with a weapon attack, target makes Fortitude save. If you win by 1+ successes, target drops one held item of your choice
- **Usage:** Once per turn

#### Battle Master: Riposte
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess, Agi 6, Melee 5
- **Benefit:** When a creature misses you with a melee attack, you can use your reaction to make one melee weapon attack against that creature
- **Usage:** Once per round (reaction)

#### Battle Master: Trip Attack
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess, Str 5, Melee 5
- **Benefit:** When you hit with a melee weapon attack, target makes Reflex save. If you win by 1+ successes, target falls Prone
- **Usage:** Once per turn

#### Brutal Critical
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess, Str 6, Melee 5
- **Benefit:** When you score a critical hit (double 1s), add your Strength score an additional time to the damage
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Colossus Slayer
- **Prerequisites:** Ranger's Path (Hunter), Wis 5
- **Benefit:** Once per turn when you hit a creature that is below its maximum HP, deal an extra 1d8 damage
- **Usage:** Once per turn

#### Companion's Strike
- **Prerequisites:** Ranger's Path (Beast Master), animal companion
- **Benefit:** When you take the Attack action, your animal companion can use its reaction to make one attack
- **Usage:** Once per round (uses companion's reaction)

#### Cunning Action
- **Prerequisites:** Roguish Training, Agi 5
- **Benefit:** You can Disengage or Hide using only 1 [Minor] action instead of a full action
- **Usage:** Unlimited

#### Defensive Fighting
- **Prerequisites:** Agi 4, Melee 3
- **Benefit:** Defensive Stance - opponents add 1 to both attack dice when attacking you
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Deflect Missiles
- **Prerequisites:** Monastic Training, Agi 5
- **Benefit:** When hit by a ranged weapon attack, use your reaction to subtract your Agility score from the damage (minimum 0)
- **Usage:** Once per round (reaction)

#### Defensive Duelist
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess, Agi 6
- **Benefit:** When wielding a finesse weapon (one that can use Agility for damage), you can use your reaction to add 2 to both of an attacker's dice against one melee attack
- **Usage:** Once per round (reaction)

#### Divine Smite
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Warrior, Positive Mastery 3
- **Benefit:** When you hit with a melee attack, you may expend Energy to add radiant damage: 2 Energy for +4 radiant, 4 Energy for +8 radiant, 6 Energy for +12 radiant. Double damage if target is undead or fiend
- **Usage:** Limited by Energy pool

#### Escape the Horde
- **Prerequisites:** Ranger's Path, Agi 6
- **Benefit:** Opportunity attacks against you have Misfortune
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Evasion
- **Prerequisites:** Roguish Training, Agi 6, Tier 3+
- **Benefit:** When subjected to an effect allowing Reflex save for half damage: if you win (1+ successes) take no damage; if you lose (0 successes) take half damage
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Frenzy
- **Prerequisites:** Rage feat, Con 6, Tier 3+
- **Benefit:** While raging, when you make your first melee attack on your turn, you may make one additional melee attack. Use the same Agility die result for both attacks, roll Melee Combat skill die separately for the second attack
- **Usage:** While raging, once per turn

#### Great Weapon Fighting
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess, Str 6, Melee 5
- **Benefit:** When wielding a two-handed melee weapon, +2 damage on all attacks
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Horde Breaker
- **Prerequisites:** Ranger's Path (Hunter), Agi 5
- **Benefit:** Once per turn when you make a weapon attack, you can make another attack with the same weapon against a different creature within 5 feet of the original target
- **Usage:** Once per turn

#### Hunter's Mark
- **Prerequisites:** Ranger's Path, Wis 4
- **Benefit:** As 1 [Minor] action, mark a creature within 60 feet for 1 minute: +4 damage when you hit the marked target, subtract 1 from both dice on Perception or Wilderness checks to track it. If target drops to 0 HP, you can mark a new target
- **Usage:** Unlimited (one marked target at a time)

#### Improved Critical
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess, chosen combat skill 6
- **Benefit:** You score a critical hit on double 1s or when both dice show 2
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Improved Divine Smite
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Smite feat, Positive Mastery 5, Tier 5+
- **Benefit:** Whenever you hit with a melee weapon, you deal an additional 4 radiant damage
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Improved Sneak Attack
- **Prerequisites:** Roguish Training, Dex 6, Tier 3+
- **Benefit:** Your Sneak Attack damage increases to Dexterity × 2
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Intimidating Presence
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess (Berserker), Cha 5
- **Benefit:** As 1 [Combat] action, choose one creature within 30 feet that can see or hear you. Target makes Will save against your Charisma + Intimidation. If you win, target is Frightened until end of your next turn
- **Usage:** Unlimited

#### Multiattack Defense
- **Prerequisites:** Ranger's Path (Hunter), Agi 5
- **Benefit:** When a creature hits you with an attack, subtract 2 from all additional attack dice from that creature until the start of your next turn
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Patient Defense
- **Prerequisites:** Monastic Training, Wis 4
- **Benefit:** As 1 [Combat] action, enter defensive stance until start of your next turn: attacks against you have Misfortune, you cannot attack while in this stance
- **Usage:** Unlimited

#### Power Attack
- **Prerequisites:** Str 4, Melee 3
- **Benefit:** Declare before rolling: add 1 to both die results and deal +1d8 damage on hit
- **Usage:** Unlimited

#### Precise Shot
- **Prerequisites:** Dex 4, Ranged 3
- **Benefit:** Ignore penalties for shooting into melee or partial cover
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Quivering Palm
- **Prerequisites:** Monastic Training, Wis 7, Tier 7+
- **Benefit:** When you hit a creature with an unarmed strike, you can spend 3 Luck to start imperceptible vibrations. Within your tier in days, you can use 1 [Combat] action to end the vibrations: target makes Fortitude save against your Wisdom + Melee Combat. If you win target drops to 0 HP; if you lose target takes damage equal to 10 × your tier
- **Usage:** Limited by Luck (one creature at a time)

#### Rage
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess (Berserker), Str or Con 5
- **Benefit:** As 1 [Combat] action, enter Rage for 1 minute: gain temporary HP equal to your Constitution score, +2 damage on melee attacks using Strength, +2 DR against physical damage, subtract 1 from both dice on Fortitude saves, cannot weave while raging
- **Usage:** Unlimited uses

#### Reckless Attack
- **Prerequisites:** Rage feat, Str 6
- **Benefit:** When you make your first attack on your turn, you may attack recklessly: you have Fortune on the attack roll and attacks against you have Fortune until the start of your next turn
- **Usage:** Unlimited

#### Second Wind
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess, Con 4
- **Benefit:** As 1 [Combat] action, regain HP equal to Constitution × 2
- **Usage:** Once per short rest

#### Shield Master
- **Prerequisites:** Str 3, Melee 3, must be using a shield
- **Benefit:** Subtract 1 from Reflex saves with shield; once per combat as a [Reaction] completely negate one attack that would hit you; treat shield as secondary weapon with Two-Weapon Fighting benefits, dealing 4 damage bludgeoning
- **Usage:** Limited (once per combat for negation)

#### Step of the Wind
- **Prerequisites:** Monastic Training, Agi 5
- **Benefit:** When you take a [Move] action: double your movement speed, jumping doesn't cost extra movement, can Disengage as part of the movement
- **Usage:** Unlimited

#### Stunning Strike
- **Prerequisites:** Monastic Training, Agi 5, Wis 5
- **Benefit:** When you hit the same creature 2+ times in one turn, you may attempt to stun: opposed Will save (your Wisdom + Melee Combat vs target's Wisdom + Luck). If you win target is Stunned until the end of your next turn
- **Usage:** Once per turn

#### Two-Weapon Fighting
- **Prerequisites:** Agi 4, Dex 3
- **Benefit:** When making dual-weapon attacks in a single [Attack] action, first attack is normal, second attack adds 1 to both die results (instead of 2). Both attacks use Shared Attribute Roll
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Uncanny Dodge
- **Prerequisites:** Roguish Training, Agi 6
- **Benefit:** When an attacker you can see hits you with an attack, you may use your reaction to add 2 to both of the attacker's attack dice (potentially turning the hit into a miss)
- **Usage:** Once per round (reaction)

#### Weapon Master
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess (Weapon Master), Str or Agi 6, chosen combat skill 6
- **Benefit:** Choose one weapon type: reduce multiple attack penalty by an additional 1 and subtract 1 from one attack die when using that weapon
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Weapon Specialization
- **Prerequisites:** Melee 4 or Ranged 4
- **Benefit:** Choose one weapon type. When attacking with that weapon, subtract 1 from your attack skill die
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Channel Divinity: Sacred Weapon
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Warrior, Positive Mastery 4
- **Benefit:** As 1 [Combat] action, imbue your weapon with divine power for 1 minute: subtract 1 from both attack dice, weapon sheds bright light 20 feet and dim light 20 feet beyond, weapon deals radiant damage instead of physical
- **Usage:** Once per short rest

#### Channel Divinity: Turn Undead
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Warrior, Positive Mastery 3
- **Benefit:** As 1 [Combat] action, present your holy symbol and speak a prayer. Each undead within 30 feet that can see or hear you must make Will save against your Charisma + Positive Mastery. If you win, undead is Frightened for 1 minute and must use movement to get as far from you as possible. Effect ends if undead takes damage
- **Usage:** Once per short rest

#### Channel Focus
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Gift, Pos or Neg Mastery 6
- **Benefit:** Choose one energy type: channel as bonus action (costs only 1 action instead of 2)
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Efficient Weaving
- **Prerequisites:** Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, any Mastery 4
- **Benefit:** Choose one energy type: weaves cost 1 less Energy (minimum 1)
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Empowered Channeling
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Gift, Pos or Neg Potential 6
- **Benefit:** Once per day when channeling: treat the effect as if twice the Energy was spent, but only spend the normal amount
- **Usage:** Once per day

#### Improved Channeling
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Gift, Wis 5, Pos or Neg Mastery 4
- **Benefit:** 40 ft radius, 1d8 per 3 Energy, Turn/Command subtract 1 from one die result
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Metaweaving: Empower
- **Prerequisites:** Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, any Mastery 5
- **Benefit:** Once per day: spend 2 extra Energy, roll damage twice, take higher result per die
- **Usage:** Once per day

#### Metaweaving: Extend
- **Prerequisites:** Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, Int or Wis 5
- **Benefit:** Once per day: double weave duration with no extra cost
- **Usage:** Once per day

#### Potent Channeling
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Gift, Pos or Neg Mastery 5
- **Benefit:** Add Wisdom to healing and necrotic damage when channeling
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Ritual Weaver
- **Prerequisites:** Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, Int or Wis 4
- **Benefit:** Use a 10-minute ritual to cast weaves at half Energy cost (minimum 1), cannot be used in combat
- **Usage:** Unlimited (out of combat only)

#### Selective Channeling
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Gift, Wis 6, Pos or Neg Mastery 5
- **Benefit:** With Heal or Harm intent: exclude up to half your Wisdom score (rounded down) targets
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Split Energy
- **Prerequisites:** Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, two Masteries 5+
- **Benefit:** Split weave to two targets or locations, each uses half Energy
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Weave Focus
- **Prerequisites:** Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift, any Mastery 3
- **Benefit:** Choose one energy type: subtract 1 from Potential and Mastery die results for casting rolls
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Wild Magic
- **Prerequisites:** Magical Aptitude, Luck 5
- **Benefit:** On double 1s or 8s when weaving: roll on Wild Magic table for extra effect
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Acrobatic Defense
- **Prerequisites:** Agi 5, Acrobatics 4
- **Benefit:** When unarmored, subtract 1 from both defense dice
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Expertise
- **Prerequisites:** Any skill rank 5
- **Benefit:** For chosen skill, subtract 1 from both dice when making checks
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Jack of All Trades
- **Prerequisites:** Int 4, five skills at 3+
- **Benefit:** Rank 1 skills count as rank 2
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Linguist
- **Prerequisites:** Int 4, Language 3
- **Benefit:** For languages known gain +2; learn new languages faster
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Medical Expert
- **Prerequisites:** Wis 4, Medicine 4
- **Benefit:** Subtract 1 from both dice on Medicine checks and heal 1d8 additional HP
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Master Craftsman
- **Prerequisites:** Dex 5, Craft 5
- **Benefit:** Work twice as fast on crafting projects and produce enhanced quality items
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Quick Learner
- **Prerequisites:** Int 5
- **Benefit:** All skill improvements cost 2 less XP
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Reliable Talent
- **Prerequisites:** Roguish Training, Tier 5+
- **Benefit:** Choose two skills in which you have rank 5+. When you roll those skills and get a result higher than your skill rank, treat it as equal to your skill rank instead
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Skill Mastery
- **Prerequisites:** Any skill rank 6
- **Benefit:** Once per session, automatically succeed with 2 successes on a skill check (no roll required) if both your attribute and skill are 4 or higher
- **Usage:** Once per session

#### Supreme Sneak
- **Prerequisites:** Roguish Training, Stealth 6
- **Benefit:** Subtract 1 from both Stealth dice when attempting to hide
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Alert
- **Prerequisites:** Wis 4, Perception 3
- **Benefit:** Initiative: +2, cannot be surprised
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Ambidextrous
- **Prerequisites:** Dex 4
- **Benefit:** No off-hand penalties
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Aura of Courage
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Warrior, Cha 5, Tier 4+
- **Benefit:** You and allies within 10 feet cannot be Frightened
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Aura of Protection
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Warrior, Cha 5, Tier 3+
- **Benefit:** You and allies within 10 feet subtract 1 from both dice on all saving throws
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Blindsense
- **Prerequisites:** Roguish Training, Perception 7, Tier 6+
- **Benefit:** You can sense the location of any hidden or invisible creature within 10 feet, as long as you can hear
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Charming
- **Prerequisites:** Cha 5, Persuasion 4
- **Benefit:** Once per session reroll failed social check; double 1s count as ally success
- **Usage:** Once per session

#### Cleansing Touch
- **Prerequisites:** Divine Warrior, Cha 6, Tier 4+
- **Benefit:** As 1 [Combat] action, touch a willing creature to end one weave effect or condition
- **Usage:** Your Charisma score times per long rest

#### Danger Sense
- **Prerequisites:** Martial Prowess (Berserker), Wis 4
- **Benefit:** Subtract 1 from both dice on Reflex saves against effects you can see
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Diamond Soul
- **Prerequisites:** Monastic Training, Wis 7, Tier 6+
- **Benefit:** Subtract 1 from both dice on all saving throws. If you fail a saving throw, you may spend 1 Luck to succeed instead
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Fast Healer
- **Prerequisites:** Con 5
- **Benefit:** Short rest: gain +Con HP; long rest: gain double HP
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Fortune's Favorite
- **Prerequisites:** Luck 5
- **Benefit:** Once per session, after seeing any roll result (yours or an enemy's), force a reroll and choose which result to use
- **Usage:** Once per session

#### Indomitable
- **Prerequisites:** Con 6, Tier 4+
- **Benefit:** Once per session: when reduced to 0 HP, instead become 1 HP and take one action
- **Usage:** Once per session

#### Iron Will
- **Prerequisites:** Wis 5
- **Benefit:** Will saves: subtract 1 from die results; once per session reroll failed Will save
- **Usage:** Once per session (reroll)

#### Natural Explorer
- **Prerequisites:** Ranger's Path, Wilderness 5
- **Benefit:** Choose one terrain type: subtract 1 from both dice on Wilderness and Perception checks in that terrain, your group can't become lost in that terrain except by magical means, you can move stealthily at normal pace in that terrain. Can be taken multiple times for different terrains
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Resilient
- **Prerequisites:** Con 5
- **Benefit:** Fortitude saves: subtract 1 from die results; success on ongoing effect ends it
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Slippery Mind
- **Prerequisites:** Roguish Training, Wis 5, Tier 5+
- **Benefit:** Subtract 1 from both dice on Wisdom saving throws
- **Usage:** Passive

#### Slow Fall
- **Prerequisites:** Monastic Training, Tier 2+
- **Benefit:** You can use your reaction when you fall to reduce falling damage by an amount equal to 5 × your tier
- **Usage:** Once per round (reaction)

#### Stillness of Mind
- **Prerequisites:** Monastic Training, Wis 5
- **Benefit:** As 1 [Combat] action, end one effect on yourself that is causing you to be Charmed or Frightened
- **Usage:** Unlimited

#### Tough
- **Prerequisites:** Con 4
- **Benefit:** Gain HP equal to Con × current Tier
- **Usage:** Passive
