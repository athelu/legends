
# D8 TTRPG Part 11: Weaves (N-Z)

## P

### Passwall
-   **Primary Energy:** Earth 6 (creating temporary passage through stone/wood, major effect)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time 2 (duration 1 minute)
-   **Total Cost:** 8 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Weaving Roll:** Earth Potential + Earth Mastery + Time Potential + Time Mastery (4d8)
-   **Effect:** Create a passage at close range (30ft) up to 5 feet wide, 8 feet tall, and 20 feet deep through wood, plaster, or stone. When weave ends, creatures still in passage are ejected safely.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 10 feet deep (1 round), 2 = full effect, 3 = 40 feet deep (10 minutes), 4 = permanent passage

### Phantasmal Force
-   **Primary Energy:** Space 4 (illusion affecting one creature's mind, mental illusion)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time 2 (duration 1 minute, concentration)
-   **Total Cost:** 6 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Weaving Roll:** Space Potential + Space Mastery + Time Potential + Time Mastery (4d8)
-   **Effect:** Create an illusion at medium range (60ft, 10ft cube) in one creature's mind. Target makes Will save. If you win, target perceives illusion as real and rationalizes illogical elements. If illusion could damage target, it takes 1d8 psychic damage per round. Target can use action + Perception vs your Space Mastery to recognize illusion.
-   **Success Scaling:** Net successes: 1 = 1 round, 2 = full effect (2d8 damage), 3 = harder to disbelieve (-1 penalty), 4 = 10 minutes or 3d8 damage

### Phantasmal Killer
-   **Primary Energy:** Space 6 (creating terrifying illusion in target's mind, major mental effect)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time 2 (duration up to 1 minute, concentration)
-   **Total Cost:** 8 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Weaving Roll:** Space Potential + Space Mastery + Time Potential + Time Mastery (4d8)
-   **Effect:** Target at medium range (60ft) makes Will save. If you win, target sees its worst fear made manifest. Target is Frightened and takes 3d8 psychic damage at start of each turn. Can repeat Will save each round to end effect.
-   **Success Scaling:** Net successes: 1 = Frightened only (no damage), 2 = full effect, 3 = 4d8 damage, 4 = Fleeing instead of Frightened (5d8 damage)

### Planar Binding
-   **Primary Energy:** Space 6 (binding extraplanar creature, major binding)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time 4 (duration 1 hour or until service completed)
-   **Total Cost:** 10 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions), takes 1 hour to cast
-   **Weaving Roll:** Space Potential + Space Mastery + Time Potential + Time Mastery (4d8)
-   **Effect:** Bind one extraplanar creature (celestial, elemental, fey, fiend) at close range (30ft) to your service. Creature makes Will save. If you win, creature must serve you for duration or until completing one task. Must negotiate payment/service.
-   **Success Scaling:** Net successes: 1 = 10 minutes service, 2 = 1 hour, 3 = 24 hours, 4 = 10 days or complex task

### Poison Spray
-   **Primary Energy:** Water 2 (toxic mist) or Negative 2 (necrotic poison)
-   **Supporting Energy:** None (touch or 10 feet, instantaneous)
-   **Total Cost:** 2 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Weaving Roll:** Water/Negative Potential + Water/Negative Mastery (2d8 only)
-   **Effect:** Target takes 8 poison damage. Target makes Fortitude save to reduce.
-   **Success Scaling:** Net successes after save: 0 = no damage, 1 = half, 2 = full (8), 3 = +8 and Poisoned, 4 = +16 and Poisoned

### Polymorph
-   **Primary Energy:** Space 6 (transforming into another creature, major transformation)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time 3 (duration up to 10 minutes, concentration)
-   **Total Cost:** 9 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Weaving Roll:** Space Potential + Space Mastery + Time Potential + Time Mastery (4d8)
-   **Effect:** Transform target at close range (30ft) into a beast. Target gains beast's HP (temporary), physical stats, and abilities. Keeps mental stats and personality. Unwilling targets get Fortitude save. Reverts when reaching 0 HP or weave ends (returns to previous HP total).
-   **Success Scaling:** Net successes (if unwilling): 1 = 1 minute, 2 = full effect, 3 = 1 hour, 4 = 8 hours

### Project Image
-   **Primary Energy:** Space 6 (creating illusory duplicate at range, complex illusion)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time 5 (medium range 60ft on creation + duration 10 minutes, concentration)
-   **Total Cost:** 11 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Weaving Roll:** Space Potential + Space Mastery + Time Potential + Time Mastery (4d8)
-   **Effect:** Create an illusory duplicate of yourself that you can see and hear through (can move up to 120 feet away). Can cast weaves from duplicate's location. Duplicate can move and speak. Physical interaction reveals it as illusion.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 minute, 2 = full effect, 3 = 1 hour, 4 = long range (240 feet, 8 hours)

### Protection from Energy
-   **Primary Energy:** Space 5 (protective barrier against specific energy, resistance)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time 3 (duration 10 minutes)
-   **Total Cost:** 8 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Weaving Roll:** Space Potential + Space Mastery + Time Potential + Time Mastery (4d8)
-   **Effect:** Target at touch gains Resistance (+1 DR) against one chosen damage type (fire, cold, acid, lightning, thunder).
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 minute, 2 = full effect, 3 = 1 hour, 4 = Greater Resistance (+2 DR, 8 hours)

## R

### Rope Trick
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (creating extradimensional space)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 7 Energy (4 base + 3 for 10 minutes)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Touch
-   **Duration:** 10 minutes
-   **Effect:** Touch a rope up to 60 feet long. Upper end rises and disappears into extradimensional space holding up to 8 Medium creatures. Creatures can pull rope up to hide. From outside, rope is invisible. Space holds up to 8 hours of air.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 minute (4 creatures), 2 = full effect, 3 = 1 hour (16 creatures), 4 = 8 hours (permanent until exited)

## S

### Scrying
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (remote viewing of specific target)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 9 Energy (6 base + 3 for 10 minutes)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions), takes 10 minutes to cast
-   **Range:** Unlimited (same plane)
-   **Duration:** 10 minutes (concentration)
-   **Effect:** Create invisible sensor near target creature you choose. Target makes Will save. Success depends on how well you know target (Fortune/Misfortune applied). If you win, you can see and hear through sensor. Target has vague feeling of being watched.
-   **Success Scaling:** Net successes: 1 = 1 minute, 2 = full effect, 3 = 1 hour, 4 = target doesn't sense you (8 hours)

### Shape Stone
-   **Primary Energy:** Earth (reshaping worked stone)
-   **Supporting Energy:** None
-   **Total Cost:** 3 Energy per 5ft cube
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Touch
-   **Effect:** Reshape stone into any form (doors, pillars, crude weapon). Can create hinges and latches. Cannot make complex mechanisms. Changes are permanent.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 2ft cube, 2 = 5ft cube, 3 = 10ft cube, 4 = 20ft cube or create detailed carvings

### Shape Water
-   **Primary Energy:** Water (manipulating water)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration for animation/color)
-   **Total Cost:** 2 Energy per effect
-   **Action:** Simple weave (1 action)
-   **Range:** Close (30 feet)
-   **Duration:** Instantaneous or 1 hour for changes
-   **Effect:** Choose one: (1) Move up to 5ft cube of water up to 5 feet, (2) Shape water into simple forms that last 1 hour, (3) Change color/opacity for 1 hour, (4) Freeze water (unfrozen creatures can escape).
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 2ft cube, 2 = 5ft cube, 3 = 10ft cube, 4 = permanent shape/color changes

### Shield
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (force barrier)
-   **Supporting Energy:** None
-   **Total Cost:** 2 Energy
-   **Action:** Reaction
-   **Duration:** Until end of your next turn
-   **Effect:** When hit by an attack or targeted by Magic Missile, gain Fortune on your defense roll (or automatically block Magic Missile). Applies to the triggering attack and any others until end of your next turn.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = triggering attack only, 2 = until end of next turn, 3 = +2 DR instead of Fortune, 4 = +4 DR (1 minute)

### Silent Image
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (visual-only illusion)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 4 Energy (2 base + 2 for 1 minute)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Medium (60 feet)
-   **Area:** 15ft cube
-   **Duration:** 1 minute (concentration)
-   **Effect:** Create a visual illusion of object, creature, or phenomenon. Can move illusion with action. No sound, smell, or other sensory elements. Physical interaction reveals illusion.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 round, 2 = full effect, 3 = 10 minutes, 4 = 30ft cube (1 hour)

### Sleep
-   **Primary Energy:** Positive (inducing magical slumber)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Space (area)
-   **Total Cost:** 4 Energy (3 base + 1 for 10ft radius)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Medium (60 feet)
-   **Area:** 10ft radius
-   **Effect:** Creatures in area make Will saves. Starting with lowest HP targets, those who fail fall Unconscious for 1 minute. Awakens if damaged or shaken (action). Doesn't affect constructs or undead.
-   **Success Scaling:** Your weaving successes vs each creature's Will save. Higher your successes = affect more/tougher creatures

### Slow
-   **Primary Energy:** Time (decelerating personal time)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Space (multiple targets)
-   **Total Cost:** 6 Energy (4 base + 2 for 4 targets)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Medium (60 feet)
-   **Duration:** 1 minute (concentration)
-   **Effect:** Up to 4 targets make Will saves. Those who fail have only 2 actions per turn (instead of 3), Misfortune on Agility checks and Reflex saves, and Misfortune on attacks. Targets can repeat save at end of each turn.
-   **Success Scaling:** Net successes: 1 = 1 round, 2 = full effect, 3 = 10 minutes, 4 = 6 targets or only 1 action per turn

### Speak with Dead
-   **Primary Energy:** Negative (animating corpse to speak)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration of questioning)
-   **Total Cost:** 6 Energy (4 base + 2 for 1 minute)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Touch
-   **Duration:** 1 minute
-   **Effect:** Grant semblance of life to corpse (dead no more than 10 days). Can ask up to 5 questions. Corpse can only answer what it knew in life. Answers are usually brief, cryptic, or repetitive. Same corpse can't be questioned this way again for 10 days.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 2 questions, 2 = 5 questions, 3 = 8 questions, 4 = corpse dead up to 100 days (10 questions)

### Spell Turning
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (redirecting magical energy)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 9 Energy (7 base + 2 for 1 minute)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Duration:** 1 minute or until expended
-   **Effect:** Next 3 weaves targeting only you are reflected back at the weaver. Weaves targeting area aren't reflected. After 3 reflections or 1 minute, effect ends.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 reflection, 2 = 3 reflections, 3 = 5 reflections (10 minutes), 4 = 8 reflections (1 hour)

### Stone Shape
-   **Primary Energy:** Earth (reshaping natural stone)
-   **Supporting Energy:** None
-   **Total Cost:** 4 Energy per 5ft cube
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Touch
-   **Effect:** Reshape natural stone into any form (crude weapons, doors, passages). More effective than Shape Stone on natural stone. Changes are permanent.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 2ft cube, 2 = 5ft cube, 3 = 10ft cube, 4 = 20ft cube

### Summon Elemental
-   **Primary Energy:** Matching elemental energy (Earth/Air/Fire/Water)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 9 Energy (6 base + 3 for 10 minutes)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Close (30 feet)
-   **Duration:** 10 minutes (concentration)
-   **Effect:** Summon an elemental matching your Primary Energy. Elemental has HP = (Your Potential Ã— 6), deals 10 damage, uses your Mastery for attacks, and has special abilities based on type. You can command it as [Free] action. If concentration breaks, elemental acts independently.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 minute, 2 = full effect, 3 = 1 hour, 4 = 8 hours or 2 elementals

## T

### Telekinesis
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (moving objects with force)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 7 Energy (4 base + 3 for 10 minutes)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions), then action each round to maintain control
-   **Range:** Medium (60 feet)
-   **Duration:** 10 minutes (concentration)
-   **Effect:** Move object up to 500 lbs up to 30 feet per round. Can use to restrain creature (opposed check: your Space Mastery vs their Athletics). Restrained creature can retry each round.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 100 lbs (1 minute), 2 = 500 lbs (full effect), 3 = 1000 lbs (1 hour), 4 = 2000 lbs and can fine manipulate (8 hours)

### Telekinetic Shove
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (force push/pull)
-   **Supporting Energy:** None
-   **Total Cost:** 2 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Close (30 feet)
-   **Effect:** Push or pull one creature/object up to 10 feet. Target makes Fortitude save to resist (if creature). Can also be used to knock prone (Reflex save).
-   **Success Scaling:** Net successes: 1 = 5 feet, 2 = 10 feet, 3 = 15 feet, 4 = 20 feet or knock prone

### Teleport
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (long-range teleportation)
-   **Supporting Energy:** None
-   **Total Cost:** 7 Energy base (more for longer distances or more creatures)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Touch (up to 8 willing creatures)
-   **Effect:** Instantly transport to destination on same plane. Success depends on familiarity: Very familiar (2 successes needed): home, place visited often; Seen casually (3 successes): seen a few times; Viewed once (4 successes): seen once or through scrying; Description only (4 successes, Misfortune): never seen, described only
-   **Failure:** Mishap (GM determines where you arrive)
-   **Success Scaling:** Roll determines accuracy: 4+ successes = exact spot, 2-3 = general area, 1 = far off target, 0 = mishap

### True Seeing
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (perceiving true forms and hidden things)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 9 Energy (6 base + 3 for 10 minutes)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Touch
-   **Duration:** 10 minutes
-   **Effect:** Target can see through illusions, invisible creatures/objects, ethereal plane, darkness (magical and normal), disguises, polymorphs, and see true forms of shapechangers. Can see into the Ethereal Plane up to 60 feet.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 minute, 2 = full effect, 3 = 1 hour, 4 = 8 hours or 120 feet ethereal sight

## V

### Vampiric Touch
-   **Primary Energy:** Negative (draining life force)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration of charge)
-   **Total Cost:** 6 Energy (4 base + 2 for 1 minute)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions) to cast, then complex weave (2 actions) to discharge
-   **Range:** Touch
-   **Duration:** 1 minute or until discharged
-   **Effect:** Your touch deals 12 necrotic damage. You heal HP equal to half damage dealt. Can hold the charge for up to 1 minute and attack multiple times if charge remains.
-   **Success Scaling:** 0 = no charge created, 1 = 8 damage (heal 4), 2 = 12 damage (heal 6), 3 = 16 damage (heal 8), 4 = 24 damage (heal 12, 10 minutes)

## W

### Wall of Fire
-   **Primary Energy:** Fire (curtain of flames)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Space (wall shape), Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 10 Energy (5 base + 2 for wall + 3 for 10 minutes)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Medium (60 feet)
-   **Duration:** 10 minutes (concentration)
-   **Effect:** Create wall of fire up to 60 feet long, 20 feet high, 1 foot thick (or ring 20 feet diameter). One side deals 16 fire damage to creatures within 10 feet. Passing through deals 24 fire damage. Provides total cover.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 minute, 2 = full effect, 3 = 1 hour, 4 = 32/40 damage (8 hours)

### Wall of Force
-   **Primary Energy:** Space (barrier of pure force)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 9 Energy (6 base + 3 for 10 minutes)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Medium (60 feet)
-   **Duration:** 10 minutes (concentration)
-   **Effect:** Create invisible wall of force up to 80 feet long, 20 feet high, 1 foot thick (or hemisphere 10 feet diameter, or sphere 10 feet diameter). Totally blocks physical and magical effects. Can't be dispelled but Disintegrate destroys it. Nothing can pass through.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 minute, 2 = full effect, 3 = 1 hour, 4 = no concentration needed (8 hours)

### Wall of Ice
-   **Primary Energy:** Water (barrier of solid ice)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Space (wall shape), Time (duration)
-   **Total Cost:** 9 Energy (5 base + 2 for wall + 2 for 1 minute)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Medium (60 feet)
-   **Duration:** 1 minute
-   **Effect:** Create wall of ice up to 60 feet long, 20 feet high, 1 foot thick (or hemisphere 10 feet diameter). Each 10ft section has HP = 12 + water Potential, DR 2 vs nonmagical damage. Creatures within area when created make Reflex save or take 8 cold damage. Wall melts naturally over time.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 round (6 HP sections), 2 = full effect, 3 = 10 minutes (double HP), 4 = 1 hour (triple HP, DR 4)

### Wall of Stone
-   **Primary Energy:** Earth (solid stone barrier)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Space (wall shape)
-   **Total Cost:** 8 Energy (6 base + 2 for wall)
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Range:** Medium (60 feet)
-   **Effect:** Create permanent wall of stone up to 60 feet long, 20 feet high, 6 inches thick (or 30 feet long and 1 foot thick). Each 10ft section has HP = 16 + Earth Potential, DR 4. Can shape into crenellations, bridges, etc.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = 1 minute duration only, 2 = permanent, 3 = double HP, 4 = triple HP and can create complex shapes

### Web
-   **Primary Energy:** Earth 4 (sticky webbing control effect)
-   **Supporting Energy:** Space 4 (medium range 60ft + area 20ft radius + duration 1 minute)
-   **Total Cost:** 8 Energy
-   **Action:** Complex weave (2 actions)
-   **Weaving Roll:** Earth Potential + Earth Mastery + Space Potential + Space Mastery (4d8)
-   **Effect:** Fill area with sticky webs anchored to surfaces. Creatures in area when created or entering area make Reflex save. Compare their successes to your weaving successes. If you have more, target becomes Restrained. Restrained creatures can use action + Athletics or Acrobatics check (2 successes needed) to escape. Webs are flammable; fire damage to 5ft area destroys webs in that section.
-   **Success Scaling:** 1 = Partial web (10ft radius, 1 round), 2 = Full effect (20ft radius, 1 minute), 3 = Enhanced (30ft radius, 10 minutes), 4 = Maximum (40ft radius, 1 hour)
