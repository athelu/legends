# Actions

All of the standard actions that are available to actors have been compiled here.

## Combat Actions
All characters have access to these combat options:

### Aid Another [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Make a skill check to help an ally with their action. Must be able to meaningfully help (GM discretion). Can aid attacks, skill checks, or other actions.
Success (1+): Your ally subtracts 1 from both dice on their next action.

### Called Shot [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Requirements:** Agility or Dexterity 4
**Effect:** You attempt to strike a weak spot or specific area of the target.  Declare before rolling. Add 1 to both attack die results
**Margin 1:** if Margin 1 would normally apply damage, then apply base damage.
**Margin 2:** Apply damage as normal for the Margin of success, but choose from the following bonuses:
- Half DR
- Apply specific condition
- Deny shield Ranged Defense [Reaction]

### Channel Divinity [Combat]
**Cost:** 2
**Requirements:** Divine Gift Trait
**Range:** 30-foot radius centered on you
**Effect:** Automatically succeeds for Heal/Harm intents (see Magic section for full details). Counts as ONE combat action for Multiple Action Penalty purposes

### Complex Weave [Combat]
**Cost:** 2
**Requirements:** Ability to weave, sufficient Energy
**Effect:** Cast a multi-energy weave using Primary + Supporting Energy. Counts as ONE combat action for Multiple Action Penalty purposes (even though it costs 2 actions). See (Magic System) for full weaving rules
**Weaving Roll:** Primary Potential + Primary Mastery + Supporting Potential + Supporting Mastery

### Disarm [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** You use your attack to strike the hand of the target in a way to make them drop a held item. Declare before making roll. Make a normal combat check.
**Margin 1:** if Margin 1 would normally apply damage, then apply base damage.
**Margin 2:** Apply damage as normal for the Margin of success, and the target drops a held item of the attacker's choice.

### Feint [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Make opposed Deception check (Charisma + Deception vs target's Empathy + Wisdom)
- **Margin 0:** no effect.
- **Margin 1:** This action does not count towards the multiple action penalty. You subtract 1 from both dice on your next [combat] action.
- **Margin 2:** This action does not count towards the multiple action penalty. You gain fortune on your next [combat] action.

### Grapple [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Requires at least one free hand. Make opposed Athletics check (Strength + Athletics vs target's Athletics, Acrobatics or Might)

### Melee Attack [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Requirements:** Target must be within your melee reach (typically 5 feet)
**Effect:** Make an opposed attack roll (Agility + Melee Combat vs opponent's Agility + Melee Combat)

### Ranged Attack [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Requirements:** Ranged weapon, target within range
**Effect:** Make an attack roll (Dexterity + Ranged Combat). Making a ranged attack while threatened (enemy within 5 feet) add 1 to both attack dice. Shooting into Melee: Add 1 to both die results 

### Ready an Action [Combat]
**Cost:** 1 or 2
**Effect:** Choose a trigger and an action. When the trigger occurs before your next turn, you can use your [Reaction] to perform the readied action. If the trigger does not occur you lose the readied action. Readied weaves that aren't used still consume Energy.

### Shove [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** You attempt to move an opponent in combat. Declare before rolling. Target must be no more than one size larger than you. Make opposed Athletics check (Strength + Athletics vs target's Athletics, Acrobatics or Might).
**Margin 2+:** Target is moved 5 feet in a direction chosen by the attacker.

### Simple Weave [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Requirements:** Ability to weave, sufficient Energy
**Range:** Touch to 5 feet maximum (no supporting energy for range)
**Effect:** Cast a single-energy weave (Primary Energy only, no Supporting Energy). See (Magic System) for full weaving rules and Simple Weave limitations
**Weaving Roll:** Primary Energy Potential + Primary Energy Mastery 

### Total Defense [Combat]
**Cost:** 3
**Effect:** Focus entirely on defense until your next turn. Cannot take any other actions this turn. All attacks made against you until the start of your next turn have Misfortune.

### Trip [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** You use your attack to strike the target in a way to make them lose their balance and fall. Declare before rolling. Target must be no more than one size larger than you. Make a normal combat check.
**Margin 1:** if Margin 1 would normally apply damage, then apply base damage.
**Margin 2:** Apply damage as normal for the Margin of success, and the target gains the prone condition

### Two-Weapon Fighting [Combat]
**Cost:** 1
**Requirements:** Wielding two weapons, off-hand weapon must have Light property
**Effect:** When wielding two weapons, you may make two attacks with a single [Combat] action. The Off-hand weapon must have the Light property. The attack uses a Shared Attribute Roll (roll attribute once, applies to both attacks). Roll skill die separately for each weapon.
- First Two-Weapon Action: First attack: Add 1 to both die results, Second attack: Add 2 to both die results
- Second Two-Weapon Action: First attack: Add 2 to both die results, Second attack: Add 3 to both die results
- Second Two-Weapon Action: First attack: Add 3 to both die results, Second attack: Add 4 to both die results

## Move Actions
Movement actions allow you to reposition, navigate terrain, and control the battlefield.

**Basic Movement Mechanics:**
- Speed: Your base movement speed is 30 feet per round (unless modified by ancestry, conditions, or abilities).
- Difficult terrain: It costs 2 feet of movement for every 1 foot traveled.
- Multiple Movements: You can take multiple [Move] actions in a turn, spending your actions as needed
- Splitting Movement: You can split your movement between other actions (move 15 feet, attack, move 15 feet more)
- Opportunity Attacks: Moving out of an enemy's reach provokes Opportunity Attacks (see Reactions). Forced movement (shoved, pushed by spells) and teleportation do not trigger an opportunity attack.
- Enemy Space: You cannot move through an enemy's space unless you Tumble Through or have special permission
- Ally Space: You may move through allies' spaces provided you do not interrupt or end your movement in their space.

### Advance [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Move up to your speed in feet. This movement may be interrupted by another action. Advancing through difficult terrain costs double. Advancing through another creature's space requires their permission or an Acrobatics check. Moving Diagonally more than once during an advance costs 7.5 ft.

### Climb [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Move along a vertical surface. Costs 2 feet of movement for every 1 foot climbed. Athletics check required for difficult climbs (smooth walls, overhangs)

### Crawl [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Move while Prone. Costs 2 feet of movement for every 1 foot crawled

### Disengage [Move]
**Cost:** 2
**Effect:** Your movement doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks for the rest of your turn. You may move up to your full speed. You cannot end your movement within melee reach of an enemy.

### Hide [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Make a Stealth check with the aid of partial cover or greater. Stealth Check is opposed by enemy's Perception.
- **Margin 1 +:** You gain the Hidden condition.

### High Jump [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Running start (10+ feet): Jump up to 3 + (Strength ÷ 2, rounded down) feet. Standing jump: Jump up to half that distance. An Athletics check may be required for difficult jumps (GM discretion), Landing in difficult terrain may require Acrobatics check to avoid falling prone

### Long Jump [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Running start (10+ feet): Jump up to your Strength score in feet. Standing jump: Jump up to half your Strength score in feet

### Mount/Dismount [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Mount or dismount a creature/vehicle. The mount must be within 5 feet and willing. Mounting/dismounting doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks.

### Squeeze [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Move through a space one size smaller than you. It costs 2 feet of movement for every 1 foot traveled. All attacks and skill checks: Add 1 to both die results. Attacks against you have Fortune.

### Stalk [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** While Hidden, you change location. Make a Stealth check opposed by enemy's Perception.
- **Margin 0:** You lose the Hidden condition, but may have concealed from cover.
- **Margin 1+:** You use cover to successfully change location while remaining hidden.

### Stand from Prone [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Remove the Prone condition. If your speed is 0, you cannot stand up. Standing up does not provoke Opportunity Attacks.

### Swim [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Move through water/ Costs 2 feet of movement for every 1 foot swum. Athletics check required for rough water, strong currents, or swimming while armored.

### Tumble Through [Move]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Move through an Enemy space. Make an Acrobatics check Opposed by enemy's Reflex save.
- **Margin 0:** Movement ends, you provoke Opportunity Attack
- **Margin 1:** You move through their space without provoking Opportunity Attacks
- **Margin 2:** You move through their space without provoking Opportunity Attacks. Subtract 1 from both dice on your next [combat] action this turn.


## Activate Actions
Activate actions involve using items, equipment, or abilities that require deliberate triggering or operation. These actions represent more complex interactions than simple manipulation.

### Activate Magic Item [Activate]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Trigger a magic item's power or property. Some items specify different action costs (may require 2 actions or be [Free]) which overrides this default action.

### Don/Doff Shield [Activate]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Equip or remove a shield. Drawing/stowing a shield requires focused effort and cannot be done as part of a [Move] action. The shield must be readily accessible (on your back, at your side, etc.)

### Drink Potion [Activate]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Consume or administer a potion to another creature to gain its effects. The unconscious/willing creature must be adjacent. Drinking a potion while threatened provokes Opportunity Attacks

### Read Scroll [Activate]
**Cost:** 2
**Requirements:** Ability to read, appropriate magical knowledge
**Effect:** Cast the spell contained in the scroll. Arcane check required. The scroll is consumed after use. Provokes Opportunity Attacks.

### Use Consumable [Activate]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Use a consumable item (oil, poison, alchemical item, etc.). Some consumables may have different action costs (see item description).

### Use Special Ability [Activate]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Use a class feature, feat, or special ability that doesn't fit other categories. If the ability specifies that it takes more than 1 action than use its stated action cost.



## Interact Actions
Interact actions involve manipulating objects, the environment, or simple physical tasks that don't require specialized skills. Many Interact actions can be combined with movement for efficiency.

### Administered Treatment
**Cost:** Performed during the short rest period.
**Effect:** At the start of the short rest, the treating ally makes a Medicine check on the patient before rest begins. A single healer can administer treatment to multiple patients during the same short rest, making one Medicine check per patient. Using a healer's kit subtracts 1 from one Medicine die. Without one, the check is made at normal difficulty.
- **1 success:** Patient regains an additional HP equal to the treating ally's Wisdom score on top of the standard short rest recovery.
- **2 successes:** Patient regains additional HP equal to the treating ally's Wisdom score + Medicine rank.

### Draw/Stow Weapon [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Draw a weapon or stow it in its sheath/holster. Can draw/stow ONE weapon as a [Free] action once per turn as part of movement. Drawing two weapons requires Quick Draw feat or two separate actions. Doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks

### Evening Treatment
**Cost:** During long rest.
**Effect:** Make a Medicine check before the patient sleeps. If self administered: Add 1 to skill die  Once per long rest, per patient. Using a healer's kit subtracts 1 from one Medicine die regardless of who is administering.
- **1 success:** Patient regains an additional HP equal to the treating character's Wisdom score during the long rest (added to the standard Constitution × 4 recovery).
- **2 successes:** Patient regains additional HP equal to the treating character's Wisdom × 2. Additionally, the patient removes one level of Exhaustion beyond the standard one removed by long rest.

### Extinguish Flames [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Put out flames on yourself or an adjacent creature. 

### Field Dressing [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Make a Medicine check on a conscious, adjacent ally. This requires a healer's kit and Medicine rank 2+. Each target may benefit from Field Dressing once per combat encounter. You may treat a number of different patients per encounter equal to your Medicine rank.
- **1 success:** Target regains HP equal to your Wisdom score.
- **2 successes:** Target regains HP equal to your Wisdom score + your Medicine rank.

### Hand Off Item [Interact]
**Cost:** Free
**Effect:** Pass an item to an adjacent willing creature. Both creatures must have at least one free hand. Recipient must be within 5 feet. Both parties must be willing. Doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks

### Interact with Object [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** A complex manipulation of an object or environmental feature. GM determines if action can be done as [Free] during movement instead of an [interact] action.

### Pick Up Item [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Retrieve a dropped or loose/unattended item. Item must be within reach (typically 5 feet). Can be done as a [Free] action during a move.

### Remove Condition [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Treat an active condition on an adjacent creature. Success thresholds and applicable conditions are listed below.

| Condition | Rank | Required Successes |
|-----------|------|--------------------|
| Bleeding (1 stack) | 1 | 1 success |
|Bleeding (all stacks) | 1 | 2 successes |
|Poisoned (Weak) | 2 | 2 successes |
| Poisoned (Strong) | 3 | 2 successes |
| Poisoned (Deadly) | 4 | 2 successes (suppresses for 4 hours; full cure requires antidote) |
| Grievously Wounded | 3 | 2 successes |
| Disease (reduce one stage) | varies by disease severity | 2 successes |

### Search [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Make a Perception or Investigate check to find something.

### Speak [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Communicate briefly with allies or enemies. 

### Stabilize [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Make a Medicine check against a creature at 0 HP or below. Require 1+ rank in Medicine.
- **1 success:** The creature stabilizes at 0 HP and is no longer dying. They remain unconscious.
- **2 successes:** As above. The creature also regains HP equal to your Wisdom score and regains consciousness.

### Take Cover [Interact]
**Cost:** 1
**Effect:** Position yourself to gain cover benefits. Must have cover available (wall, boulder, etc.). Can be done as a [Free] action during a move.

### Use Tool/Kit [Interact]
**Cost:** Variable (typically 1-10)
**Effect:** Use thieves' tools, healer's kit, artisan's tools, etc. Time requirements vary by task complexity. Will require skill checks (Devices for locks, Medicine for healer's kit, etc.)

## Free Actions
Free actions require no significant time or effort and can be performed alongside other actions.
**Free Action Limits**
You can take a reasonable number of [Free] actions per turn (typically 1-3), subject to GM discretion.  GM may rule that excessive free actions (like dropping 20 items) require actual action costs. Free actions can typically be taken at any point during your turn.

### Drop Item [Free]
**Cost:** Free
**Effect:** Release an item from your hand. Item falls in your space. Doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks

### Drop Prone [Free]
**Cost:** Free
**Effect:** Fall prone in your current space. Doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks. Enemies have Fortune on melee attacks against you while prone.

### Release Grapple [Free]
**Cost:** Free
**Effect:** Remove the Grappled condition from yourself and the target you were grappling.

### Signal [Free]
**Cost:** Free
**Effect:** Shout an Alarm, point out a subject, or other very quick communication. Can only be done once per turn as a [Free] Action. Longer communication requires an [Interact] action.

## Reactions
Reactions are special responses triggered by specific circumstances that occur outside your turn.
**Basic Reaction Mechanics:**
You can take ONE reaction per round. Reactions occur immediately when their trigger happens. Reactions happen during another actor’s turn. You can use your reaction even before your first turn in combat (unless Surprised). Reactions don't count against your 3 actions per turn.
If you have access to multiple reactions, you must choose which one to use when the trigger occurs. Some feats, equipment, and weaves grant additional reaction options beyond those listed here. When multiple creatures have reactions that could trigger simultaneously, the reacting creature closest to the triggering creature goes first. If equidistant, the highest Initiative Bonus acts first.

### Counterweave [Combat]
**Requirements:** Ability to weave, Space energy available
**Trigger:** You see a creature weaving within medium range (60ft)
**Effect:** Attempt to counter the weave (see Counterweave in Weaves). Requires successful Arcane check to identify the weave first

### Opportunity Attack [Combat] (Reaction)
**Trigger:** A hostile creature you can see moves out of your melee reach
**Effect:** Make one melee attack against the triggering creature. Movement within your reach doesn't trigger this reaction. Teleportation and forced movement don't trigger this reaction. The Disengage action prevents opportunity attacks.

### Ranged Defense [Reaction]
**Trigger:** You are targeted by a Ranged attack while wielding a shield.
**Effect:** Force Misfortune on one ranged attack per round.

### Shield Block [Reaction]
**Trigger:** You are hit by an attack while wielding a shield
**Effect:** Once per round, Gain +6 DR against this attack only
