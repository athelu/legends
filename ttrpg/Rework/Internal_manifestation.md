## 1. Simple vs Complex Internal Manifestation

| Type | Action Cost | Multiple Action Penalty | Roll Required | Energy Cost |
|------|------------|------------------------|---------------|-------------|
| Simple | 1 action | No | No — spend energy, effect happens | Primary only |
| Complex | 1 action | No | Yes — Primary Potential + Primary Mastery | Primary + Secondary |

**Primary energy cost:** +1 above the equivalent external effect at the same magnitude. This offsets the removal of range and area costs that apply to external weaving.

**Secondary energy cost:** Duration only. Range and area costs do not apply to internal manifestation. The target is always the self.

---

## 2. Internal Manifestation Roll

Used for all Complex Internal Manifestation. Roll Primary Potential + Primary Mastery (2d8).

| Roll Result | Effect Achieved |
|-------------|----------------|
| 0 successes | Energy spent, no effect |
| 1 success | Reduced effect — one tier below energy investment |
| 2 successes | Full effect as per energy investment |
| 3 successes | Enhanced effect — one tier above energy investment, capped at table maximum |

**Overspending:** Spending beyond Mastery in any energy adds +1 to both dice on the roll per point over Mastery. Cannot spend beyond Potential — this is the absolute ceiling.

---

## 3. Internal Duration Table

Secondary energy cost determines duration for Complex Internal Manifestation effects. Range and area costs from the external weaving tables do not apply internally.

| Secondary Energy Cost | Duration |
|----------------------|---------|
| 0 | Instantaneous — no duration investment required |
| 1 | 1 round |
| 2 | 1 minute |
| 3 | 10 minutes |
| 4 | 1 hour |

The secondary energy's identity shapes the character of the duration consistent with the external system:
- **Earth secondary:** Once established, difficult to end prematurely — feels permanent for its duration
- **Fire secondary:** Burns until fuel exhausted, intensity may decrease near the end
- **Water secondary:** Seeps in and persists by saturation
- **Air secondary:** Pulses and fluctuates, sustained but variable
- **Positive secondary:** Actively renewed, ends cleanly when investment runs out
- **Negative secondary:** Accumulates in character over the duration, grows more noticeable the longer it runs
- **Space secondary:** Held in place by spatial anchoring, ends abruptly when anchor breaks
- **Time secondary:** The natural home of duration — Time as secondary means the full duration column is in play

## 4. Expression Sub-Table

Used when a pairing channels energy through weapon attacks or unarmed strikes. Bonus damage is added to existing weapon damage — it does not replace it. DR rules apply separately to each damage component.

| Primary Cost | Bonus Damage | Condition on Margin 2+ |
|-------------|-------------|----------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 of energy type | Minor condition |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 of energy type | Moderate condition |
| 7 (6+1) | +8 of energy type | Major condition |

**Negative energy expression cap:** Pairings where Negative energy is the primary or secondary damage type cap bonus damage at +4 at all cost tiers. The secondary effects at 7 cost — attribute reduction, cumulative DR reduction, condition stacking — compensate for the reduced damage ceiling.

### DR Rules for Expression Damage

| Damage Type | DR Application |
|-------------|---------------|
| Energy damage (Fire, Negative, Positive, Air-sonic) | Half DR |
| Physical damage (bludgeoning, slashing, piercing) | Full DR |
| Dual damage types | Each component calculated separately against relevant DR |
| Effects reaching the pneuma directly (Negative/Space) | No DR |

### Condition Scale by Energy Type

**Fire expression conditions** follow the fire condition stack:

| Margin | Condition | Effect |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Margin 2+ at 3 cost | Singed | Add 1 to ability die on target's next action. Ends after their next action. |
| Margin 2+ at 5 cost | Smoldering | Target takes 2 fire damage at the start of their next turn only. |
| Margin 2+ at 7 cost | Ignited | Target takes 4 fire damage at the start of each turn. Requires 1 action to extinguish. |

Burning (Level 4) is not achievable through Expression effects. Expression adds energy to a strike — it does not engulf the target. Burning is external weave territory.

Existing fire condition stacking rules apply. A target already Smoldering who takes an Ignited-level hit upgrades to Ignited rather than stacking two Smoldering effects.

**Negative energy expression conditions:**

| Margin | Condition at Cost Tier |
|--------|----------------------|
| Margin 2+ at 3 cost | Weakened |
| Margin 2+ at 5 cost | Weakened + target DR reduced by 2 until end of scene |
| Margin 2+ at 7 cost | Weakened + target DR reduced by 3 until end of scene |

**Physical expression conditions (Earth primary, bludgeoning):**

| Margin | Condition at Cost Tier |
|--------|----------------------|
| Margin 2+ at 3 cost | Target DR reduced by 1 until end of scene |
| Margin 2+ at 5 cost | Target DR reduced by 2 until end of scene |
| Margin 2+ at 7 cost | Target DR reduced by 3 until end of scene |

**Poison delivery conditions (Water/Negative, Negative/Water):**

| Cost Tier | Poison Severity on Failed Save |
|-----------|-------------------------------|
| 3 cost | Weak poison |
| 5 cost | Strong poison |
| 7 cost | Deadly poison |

Poison delivery requires a Fortitude save against the internal roll result. Delivery requires physical contact — unarmed strike, weapon strike, or grapple. Barriers prevent delivery.

## 5. Life-Leach Sub-Table

Available on selected Negative primary Expression pairings and noted Fire primary pairings at higher Mastery investment. Returns flat HP per successful strike. Limited to living targets only — draining constructs, undead, or non-living matter produces no return.

| Primary Cost | HP Returned per Strike |
|-------------|----------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | 1 HP |
| 5 (4+1) | 2 HP |
| 7 (6+1) | 4 HP |

Life-leach and standard Expression bonus damage are not combined on the same strike unless the pairing entry specifically permits it. Life-leach is an alternative expression of the same energy investment, not an addition to it.

---

## 6. HP Cost Mechanic

Several Negative primary pairings offer an HP cost option alongside standard energy investment. This represents deliberate self-sacrifice — burning the character's own vitality as fuel for a more powerful effect.

**Standard HP cost:** 4 HP flat per activation. Not scaling. Not reduced by DR or any defensive effect — this cost is paid directly from current HP.

**What the HP cost buys:** One tier upgrade on the relevant Expression or effect table without spending additional energy.

| Standard Investment | Effect with HP Cost |
|--------------------|-------------------|
| 3 primary cost | Performs as 5 primary cost |
| 5 primary cost | Performs as 7 primary cost |
| 7 primary cost | Presence activates (where applicable) |

**HP cost always triggers an Exhaustion check** regardless of total energy spent that scene. The combination of energy expenditure and deliberate physical sacrifice constitutes significant self-expenditure regardless of pool totals.

HP lost to the HP cost mechanic is standard damage — it recovers normally through rest and healing. It is not life drain and does not reduce maximum HP.

## 7. Presence Resolution Framework

Presence effects radiate outward from the character as a consequence of sustained internal energy expression. They are not weaves — they require no targeting roll and cannot be countered as weaves can. They are the body becoming something that affects nearby creatures as a side effect of what the character is doing to themselves.

### Presence Activation

Presence effects are noted in individual pairing entries. Most require a minimum primary energy investment (typically 7 cost) and a minimum Mastery threshold in the secondary energy (typically 3-4). Some pairings unlock Presence through the HP cost mechanic regardless of Mastery threshold.

### Save Type by Primary Energy

The nature of the Presence effect determines which save is required:

| Primary Energy | Save Type | Rationale |
|---------------|-----------|-----------|
| Fire | Will | Psychological — the hunger and consuming intensity affect the mind |
| Air | Fortitude | Physical — life force and breath being drained from the body |
| Earth | Fortitude | Physical — material degradation affects the body directly |
| Water | Fortitude | Physical — biological contamination and sickness |
| Positive | None (beneficial) | Positive Presence aids allies — no save required |
| Negative | Fortitude or Will | Fortitude for physical drain, Will for fear and psychological effects |
| Space | Will | Psychological — the wrongness of distorted space affects the mind |
| Time | Fortitude | Physical — temporal compression forces biological aging |

### Presence Resolution

The internal manifestation roll result serves as the effective targeting result against which affected creatures save.

Creatures within range make the appropriate save each round they remain in range. The character does not spend additional actions to maintain the effect — it radiates passively for the duration of the internal manifestation.

| Save Result vs Internal Roll | Outcome |
|-----------------------------|---------|
| Target succeeds | No effect this round. Must save again next round if still in range. |
| Target fails by net 0 | Minimum effect as listed in pairing entry |
| Target fails by net 1 | Standard effect as listed |
| Target fails by net 2+ | Enhanced effect at GM discretion |

### Presence Range by Investment

Individual pairing entries specify range. General guidance:

| Primary Cost | Typical Range |
|-------------|--------------|
| 3 (2+1) | 10 feet |
| 5 (4+1) | 15–20 feet |
| 7 (6+1) | 20–30 feet |

### Presence and Exhaustion

All Presence effects trigger an Exhaustion check at the end of the scene regardless of total energy spent. Radiating energy outward from the self while sustaining internal expression is the most demanding thing the body does in this system. There are no exceptions to this rule.

Positive/Space Presence (the only beneficial Presence effect) still triggers the Exhaustion check — sustaining an outward field is costly regardless of its intent.

## 8. Exhaustion Check Framework

Internal manifestation routes energy through the body directly. This wears the body down in ways external weaving does not. External weaving reaches outward through the pneuma — the body is not the conduit. Internal manifestation uses the body as the conduit. The Exhaustion check represents that cost.

### When a Check Is Required

At the end of any scene or encounter where Complex Internal Manifestation was used:

| Situation | Check Required | Difficulty |
|-----------|---------------|-----------|
| Total energy spent within highest Mastery rank | No | — |
| Total energy spent exceeded highest Mastery rank | Yes | 1 success |
| Any single use overspent beyond Mastery | Yes | 1 success |
| Any Presence effect used | Yes | 1 success |
| Any HP cost activation | Yes | 1 success |
| Time/Space or Space/Time at 5+ cost | Yes | 2 successes |
| Fire/Time at 7 cost | Yes | 1 success |
| Earth/Time at 7 cost | Yes | 1 success |
| Time/Fire at 7 cost | Yes | 1 success |

Multiple triggers in one scene do not stack into a harder check — the character makes one check at the highest applicable difficulty.

### Fortitude Save Results

**Standard difficulty (1 success required):**

| Result | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| 2 successes | No Exhaustion gained |
| 1 success | No Exhaustion gained, but cannot use Complex Internal Manifestation again until short rest |
| 0 successes | Gain one Exhaustion level |

**Elevated difficulty (2 successes required):**

| Result | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| 2 successes | No Exhaustion gained |
| 1 success | Gain one Exhaustion level |
| 0 successes | Gain two Exhaustion levels |

### Exhaustion Levels Reference

Exhaustion from internal manifestation stacks with all other Exhaustion sources — forced march, sleep deprivation, environmental exposure, and other abilities that cause Exhaustion.

| Level | Name | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| 1 | Fatigued | Add 1 to attribute die on all skill checks. No HP recovery from short rest. |
| 2 | Exhausted | Add 2 to attribute die. Half movement. No short rest benefit. |
| 3 | Severely Exhausted | Add 2 to both dice on all rolls. Half movement. Only 2 actions per turn. |
| 4 | Near Collapse | Add 3 to both dice. Quarter movement. 1 action only. No reactions. |
| 5 | Collapse | Unconscious. |

### Exhaustion and Internal Manifestation Interaction

**Fire/Earth suppression:** Fire/Earth internal manifestation suppresses Exhaustion penalties by treating the current level as lower for the duration. Suppression does not remove Exhaustion — it hides its cost. When the effect ends, full penalties return immediately.

| Fire/Earth Cost | Suppression |
|----------------|------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Treat current Exhaustion as one level lower |
| 5 (4+1) | Treat current Exhaustion as two levels lower |
| 7 (6+1) | Ignore all Exhaustion penalties for duration + no Exhaustion check for this specific activation |

The 7 cost exemption applies only to the Fire/Earth activation itself. Any other internal manifestation used the same scene still triggers checks normally.

## 9. Unstable Pairing Resolution

Two pairings — Positive/Negative and Negative/Positive — place the most fundamentally opposed energies in direct tension within the body. Standard tables do not apply. GM adjudicates outcome based on relative investment.

### Investment Assessment

| Investment State | Definition |
|-----------------|-----------|
| Balanced | Primary and secondary investment within 1 point of each other |
| Primary dominates | Primary investment exceeds secondary by 2 or more points |
| Secondary dominates | Secondary investment exceeds primary by 2 or more points |

### Outcome by Investment State

| State | Outcome |
|-------|---------|
| Balanced | Fortitude save difficulty 2. On failure: 1 Exhaustion level immediately. Effect unpredictable — GM determines outcome, which may be beneficial, harmful, or both. |
| Primary dominates | Amplified primary energy effect — equivalent pairing at one tier above normal investment. Fortitude save difficulty 1 still applies. |
| Secondary dominates | Character takes HP damage equal to secondary investment spent. GM determines a secondary energy effect. |

**Exhaustion:** Unstable pairings always trigger an Exhaustion check regardless of outcome or investment. The check uses the balanced state difficulty (2 successes) regardless of which energy dominates.
