# Positive — Internal Manifestation

## Overview

Positive internally is lifeforce directed inward. Healing, cleansing, endurance, and the body's relationship with its own vitality. Where Water/Positive produces persistent recovery through a secondary medium, Positive primary is direct — the character is channeling raw lifeforce into themselves.

Positive has the strongest healing output of any primary energy, the best condition removal, and significant anti-undead and anti-daemon utility when expressed outward through Presence. Its weakness is offense — a character built around Positive internal manifestation survives and supports but does not threaten.

One important design note: Positive primary internal manifestation is the most socially legible of all the expressions. A character visibly healing themselves in the middle of combat is recognizable as something divine users do. This creates an interesting social dynamic — Positive internal manifestation may be mistaken for divine expression by observers who don't know better, which can work for or against a character depending on context.

## Pairings

---

### Positive / Fire
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — purification and condition burning
**Expression:** Sacred flame turned inward — Positive energy carried by Fire burns corruption out of the body with heat rather than cleansing it gently. More aggressive than Positive/Water recovery, faster acting, shorter duration. The body runs hot while this is active.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Remove one active poison or disease condition immediately, +2 Fortitude saves for duration | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | Remove all active poison conditions, reduce disease by one severity stage, +2 Fortitude saves | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + immune to new poison and disease effects for duration, suppress one Exhaustion level | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Tell:** Skin flushed and hot to the touch. Fever quality to the eyes. Visible heat shimmer briefly at activation. Faint smell of ozone.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. One of the fastest condition-clearing options in the system — valuable mid-combat. Strong non-combat utility for post-exposure disease prevention.

---

### Positive / Air
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — mental and sensory restoration
**Expression:** Healing breath — Positive energy carried on Air restores clarity of mind and sense. Where Air/Positive suppresses mental conditions through cleansing wind, Positive/Air heals the underlying cause. Air/Positive is faster and combat-oriented. Positive/Air is more thorough with stronger non-combat application for genuine recovery from trauma and sense damage.

At 5+ cost sustained over multiple long rests, permanent sense damage from non-magical injury may partially or fully recover at GM discretion.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Remove one mental condition (Dazed, Disoriented, Frightened) entirely | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | Remove all mental conditions, restore one lost sense (Deafened, Blinded) for duration | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + immunity to mental conditions and sense-removing effects for duration | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Tell:** Deep deliberate breath at activation. Tension leaves the face and posture. Eyes refocus. Breathing becomes slower, fuller, more present.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Strong non-combat recovery application for trauma and sustained sense impairment.

### Positive / Earth
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — physical restoration and structural reinforcement
**Expression:** Physical restoration — Positive energy grounded through Earth rebuilds the body's structural integrity directly. Bone, muscle, and dense tissue. This is the closest internal manifestation comes to direct HP restoration, and it is the most physically visible of the Positive primary expressions.

HP regained scales with Positive Mastery rank directly — investment in Positive Mastery improves healing output. A character with Positive Mastery 5 regains 15 HP at 7 cost.

Activating Positive/Earth during a short rest adds the HP regained to the short rest recovery total on top of standard Constitution recovery.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Regain HP equal to Positive Mastery rank immediately | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | Regain HP equal to Positive Mastery rank × 2, remove Grievously Wounded condition | 1 — 1 round |
| 7 (6+1) | Regain HP equal to Positive Mastery rank × 3, remove Grievously Wounded, +2 DR all types for duration | 2 — 1 minute |

**Tell:** Wounds visibly close — not instantly but rapidly. Bruising fades. The character straightens as structural damage repairs. Brief golden quality to the skin at the point of healing.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. The most direct HP restoration expression in the internal manifestation system.

### Positive / Water
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — deep persistent recovery
**Expression:** Deep healing that persists and builds — Positive energy carried through Water saturates the body's recovery processes. Slower to activate than Positive/Earth but sustains longer and produces more total HP recovery over a full scene. The body becomes a system that heals itself continuously rather than in a single burst.

At 7 cost active during a long rest, the character recovers HP equal to Constitution × 6 instead of the standard × 4, and removes two Exhaustion levels instead of one.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Regain 1 HP per round, suppress one Bleeding stack per round | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Regain 2 HP per round, suppress all Bleeding, +1 to Fortitude saves | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | Regain 3 HP per round, suppress all Bleeding, +2 Fortitude saves, remove one poison severity stage per minute, remove one disease severity stage once per scene | 4 — 1 hour |

**Tell:** Slow visible improvement in color and alertness over the duration. Wounds close gradually. The character moves more freely as the duration continues. Faint warmth emanates from the skin.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Strongest total recovery output in the system at 7 cost over a full scene.

### Positive / Negative
**Category:** Unstable — GM adjudicates
**Table:** No standard table applies. Outcome determined by relative investment and Mastery in both energies.
**Expression:** The two most fundamentally opposed energies channeled simultaneously through the body. This pairing exists at the absolute edge of what internal manifestation can do and should not be attempted without significant Mastery in both energies.

**Balanced investment** (within 1 point of each other): The energies destabilize. The character makes a Fortitude save at difficulty 2 or gains one Exhaustion level immediately regardless of scene total. The effect produced is unpredictable — the GM determines the outcome, which may be beneficial, harmful, or both.

**Positive dominates** (Positive investment significantly exceeds Negative): Positive energy prevails and produces an amplified healing burst — Positive/Earth effect at one tier above normal investment. The Fortitude save still applies.

**Negative dominates** (Negative investment significantly exceeds Positive): The character takes HP damage equal to the Positive investment spent, but gains a powerful Negative primary effect determined by the GM.

**Exhaustion:** Always triggers Exhaustion check regardless of outcome or investment.
**Tell:** Unpredictable. Light and shadow simultaneously. Wounds opening and closing. A quality of wrongness that observers find deeply disturbing regardless of outcome.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Situational. Not recommended for deliberate use.

### Positive / Space
**Category:** Resilience with Presence at higher investment
**Table:** Buff/Debuff for protective effects. Presence uses internal roll as save target.
**Expression:** Healing at the boundary of the self — Positive energy anchored spatially creates a protective field that extends slightly beyond the body. At higher investment the character becomes a source of restorative presence that benefits those nearby.

At 7 cost with Space Mastery 3+, Presence activates: allies within 20 feet regain 1 HP at the start of each round while in range. This is the only internal manifestation Presence effect that benefits rather than harms nearby creatures. If the character is knocked unconscious the Presence effect ends immediately.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 DR all damage types, regain 1 HP per round | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 DR all damage types, regain 1 HP per round, immune to Grievously Wounded | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + Presence: allies within 20 feet regain 1 HP per round | 4 — 1 hour |

**Exhaustion:** Always triggers Exhaustion check when Presence is active, even for beneficial Presence.
**Tell:** A warmth others can feel when standing near the character. Faint golden-white quality to the light immediately around them. Animals and children are instinctively drawn toward them. Observers will likely identify this as divine expression.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. The Presence effect has significant non-combat utility — sustained activation during rest aids recovery for the whole party.

---

### Positive / Time
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — accelerated recovery
**Expression:** Accelerated recovery — Positive energy directed through Time compresses the body's natural healing processes into moments. Wounds that would take weeks close in hours. Exhaustion that would require a full night clears in minutes. This is not miraculous healing — it is the body's own processes running faster than they should.

**Diminishing returns:** Each use of Positive/Time beyond the first in the same scene increases the primary energy cost by 1. The body cannot be accelerated indefinitely. This resets after a long rest.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Reduce one condition's duration by half (minimum 1 round) | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | Remove one Exhaustion level immediately, regain HP equal to Positive Mastery rank | 1 — 1 round |
| 7 (6+1) | Remove two Exhaustion levels immediately, regain HP equal to Positive Mastery × 2, remove one disease severity stage | 2 — 1 minute |

**Tell:** Minor wounds close visibly faster than normal. Bruising fades in seconds. A brief sense of the character moving slightly out of phase — not spatially, but temporally, as if slightly ahead of the present moment.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. The most powerful Exhaustion recovery available through internal manifestation without requiring rest. Significant for extended overland travel and multi-encounter days.
