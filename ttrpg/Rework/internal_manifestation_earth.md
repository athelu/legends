# Earth — Internal Manifestation

## Overview

Earth internally is the most physical of all primaries. Stone, density, structural integrity, resistance to change. Where Fire consumes and Air flows, Earth endures. The character channeling Earth internally becomes harder, heavier, more permanent. The risk is rigidity — Earth resists not just damage but adaptation, and a character locked into Earth expression can find themselves unable to respond to what Fire or Air would handle easily.

Earth has the strongest physical Resilience expressions of any primary energy, the best DR bonuses, and the most reliable non-combat utility through material interaction. It also has the least Presence capability — Earth does not radiate outward easily.

## Pairings

---

### Earth / Fire
**Category:** Expression
**Table:** Expression sub-table
**Expression:** Molten force channeled through strikes. Earth heated by Fire produces impacts that carry both physical mass and thermal energy — the crack of stone and the sear of heat simultaneously. Unlike Fire primary expressions which are primarily thermal, Earth/Fire expression damage is split between physical bludgeoning and fire components.

**DR:** Dual damage type. Bludgeoning component applies full DR. Fire component applies half DR. Each is calculated separately against relevant DR.

| Primary Cost | Bonus Bludgeoning | Bonus Fire | Condition | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|------------------|-----------|-----------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +1 | +1 fire | Singed on margin 2+ | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | +2 | +2 fire | Smoldering on margin 2+ | 1 — 1 round |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 | +4 fire | Ignited on margin 2+ | 2 — 1 minute |

**Tell:** Striking surfaces become visibly dense and heavy. Heat radiates from fists or weapon contact points. Ground cracks slightly under the character's feet during activation.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Combat primarily. Material heating and shaping has minor non-combat utility at GM discretion.

### Earth / Air
**Category:** Enhancement
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — Strength
**Expression:** Stone given velocity. Earth channeled through Air produces raw physical force — the character's strikes carry the momentum of falling rock, their throws cover impossible distances, their physical presence becomes overwhelming. Pure Strength enhancement with no thermal component and no subtlety.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 Strength for physical checks and damage | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 Strength for physical checks and damage | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 Strength + attacks that hit at margin 2+ also apply Shove (free, no action required) | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Tell:** Footsteps heavier than they should be. Minor ground disturbance — dust settling, small objects shifting. The character's weight seems to increase noticeably.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Strength enhancement applies directly to Athletics checks, carrying capacity, and physical labor during downtime.

### Earth / Water
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — DR and physical absorption
**Expression:** Earth saturated with Water becomes a medium that absorbs and disperses force rather than simply resisting it. The character does not become harder — they become more capable of taking hits without being moved or broken by them. Damage reduction through absorption rather than deflection.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 DR against physical (bludgeoning, slashing, piercing) damage | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 DR against physical damage, reduce forced movement by 10 feet | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 DR against physical damage, immune to forced movement, reduce fall damage by half | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Tell:** Movement becomes slightly slower and more deliberate. The character's body seems to settle into the ground when standing still, as if rooting.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Environmental protection against falling, crushing hazards, and structural collapse has significant non-combat application.

### Earth / Positive
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — structural reinforcement and recovery
**Expression:** Consecrated stone — Earth reinforced by Positive energy strengthens the physical body at a structural level. Bone, cartilage, and dense tissue become more resilient. This is not healing in the sense of restoring lost HP — it reinforces the body against damage that would reduce HP in the first place. Outside combat, it accelerates natural knitting of physical injuries.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 DR against all damage types | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 DR against all damage types, +2 to Fortitude saves | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 DR all types, +2 Fortitude saves, regain 2 HP at start of each turn | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Non-Combat:** At 5+ cost, reduces healing time for physical injuries — bone fractures and structural damage heal in half the normal time during downtime recuperation.
**Tell:** Skin takes on a slightly gray, stone-like quality at the surface. Joints sound dense when moved. Minor surface cuts close more slowly — the body is investing in density, not surface repair.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. One of the strongest defensive pairings in the system.

### Earth / Negative
**Category:** Expression with Presence at higher investment
**Table:** Expression sub-table. Presence uses internal roll as save target.
**Expression:** Structural decay channeled through strikes. Earth carrying Negative energy causes what it touches to weaken and degrade — armor becomes brittle, bone micro-fractures, material integrity fails. Strikes deal physical damage with a debilitating secondary effect rather than energy damage.

**DR:** Physical bludgeoning damage — full DR applies. The decay effect is secondary and not subject to DR.

At 7+ cost with Negative Mastery 4+, Presence activates: the character's proximity causes materials and structures to subtly degrade. Objects held by nearby enemies become unreliable.

| Primary Cost | Bonus Damage | Secondary Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 bludgeoning | Target's DR reduced by 1 until end of their next turn | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 bludgeoning | Target's DR reduced by 2 until end of their next turn | 1 — 1 round |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 bludgeoning | Target's DR reduced by 2 + Weakened condition | 2 — 1 minute |

**Presence (7+ cost, Negative Mastery 4+ required):** Objects and weapons held by creatures within 20 feet become unreliable. Affected creatures must succeed on a Fortitude save against the internal roll result or their weapon or shield provides no benefit until the start of their next turn. The item does not break — it fails in that moment only.

**Exhaustion:** Always triggers Exhaustion check when Presence is active.
**Tell:** Faint smell of old stone and dust. Cracks appear briefly in surfaces touched. Metal handled by the character tarnishes visibly within minutes.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Material degradation has non-combat application — accelerating decay of structures, aging objects deliberately, identifying weaknesses in construction.

### Earth / Space
**Category:** Adaptation
**Table:** Transformation table — spatial anchoring and material sense
**Expression:** Earth anchored to a specific location in space. The character develops an extraordinary relationship with the physical environment — knowing its composition, structure, and weaknesses by contact or proximity. The character can also anchor themselves spatially, becoming extraordinarily difficult to displace by any means.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Stonecunning — determine composition, structural integrity, and approximate age of stone or metal by touch | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | As above + spatial anchor: cannot be teleported or planarly displaced against will | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + sense structural weaknesses in all objects and constructions within 30 feet, know exact composition without requiring touch | 4 — 1 hour |

**Tell:** Hands drift toward surfaces. The character pauses and seems to listen to walls and floors. A quality of stillness that reads as being *placed* rather than merely standing.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Stonecunning and structural sense are primarily non-combat — invaluable for dungeon navigation, identifying traps in stonework, evaluating construction, finding concealed passages. The spatial anchor has strong combat application against weavers who use displacement effects.

### Earth / Time
**Category:** Adaptation
**Table:** Transformation table — geological compression
**Expression:** Geological force compressed into moments. Earth and Time together produce the most physically transformative internal manifestation in the Earth primary set — the character channels the slow inevitability of stone into their body, becoming temporarily closer to geological matter than living tissue. At higher investment this approaches voluntary partial petrification — not a condition imposed from outside, but a deliberate state the character controls and can end.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Skin hardens to stone-like density: +4 DR against physical damage, movement reduced by 10 feet | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | As above + immune to Bleeding, Poisoned (Weak), and Sickened conditions for duration | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | Full stone-density: +6 DR all damage types, immune to Bleeding/Poisoned/Sickened, immune to critical hits for duration, movement reduced by 15 feet | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Exhaustion:** The 7 cost tier always triggers an Exhaustion check regardless of total energy spent that scene. Compressing geological time through living tissue is the most extreme physical transformation in the Earth primary set.
**Tell:** Skin visibly takes on stone texture and coloration during activation. Movement noticeably heavier and slower. Voice resonates differently — deeper, with less breath quality.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Combat primarily. Stone-density state has non-combat utility for enduring environmental extremes — crushing pressure, extreme cold, vacuum — where normal tissue would fail.
