# Air — Internal Manifestation

## Overview

Air internally is movement, pressure, vibration, and heightened sensitivity. Where Fire pushes outward with consuming force, Air moves with and through — enhancing reaction, perception, and precision. The risk of Air is diffusion: spread too thin, the effect loses focus entirely.

---

## Pairings

---

### Air / Fire
**Category:** Enhancement
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — Initiative and reaction time
**Expression:** Air supercharged by Fire produces explosive reactive speed. Not sustained movement enhancement — this is the body's ability to *respond* faster than normal. Heightened combat awareness, faster initiative, reactions that border on precognition.

Note the distinction from Fire/Air: Fire/Air is an explosive movement burst driven by hunger. Air/Fire is a reactive speed enhancement driven by heightened sensitivity. Same energies, different primary, meaningfully different character.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 to Initiative, subtract 1 from one Reflex save die this scene | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 to Initiative, subtract 1 from both Reflex save dice | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 Initiative, Fortune on Reflex saves, one additional Reaction per round | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Tell:** Constant subtle movement even while standing still. Eyes track faster than normal. Hair moves in a sourceless personal wind.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Primarily combat. Strong ambush detection and perception utility outside combat.

### Air / Earth
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — physical stability
**Expression:** Air compressed and grounded through Earth. The character becomes difficult to move, knock down, or destabilize. Not the immovable hardness of Earth primary — this is dynamic stability, the ability to flow and still not fall.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Immunity to Prone from physical sources (shoves, trips, knockdowns) | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | As above + cannot be moved involuntarily (immune to Shove, forced movement) | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + +2 to all Fortitude saves | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Tell:** Feet find purchase on impossible surfaces. Movement is unnaturally sure-footed. No stumbling on difficult terrain.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Traversing unstable terrain, working at height, resisting environmental forces.

### Air / Water
**Category:** Adaptation
**Table:** Transformation table
**Expression:** Air and Water combine internally to produce cold adaptation and environmental sensitivity. The character's body adjusts to temperature and pressure extremes. Secondary effect: sensing pressure changes in air and water, detecting movement, approaching weather, and hidden creatures by disturbance.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Ignore environmental cold penalties, sense pressure disturbances within 15 feet | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Ignore cold conditions up to Frosted (Level 3), pressure sense 30 feet | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | Immune to all cold conditions, pressure sense 60 feet, detect invisible creatures by air disturbance | 4 — 1 hour |

**Tell:** Breath does not mist in cold. Skin stays dry regardless of humidity. A quality of stillness that reads as alertness to observers.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Significant non-combat utility for weather prediction, detecting pursuit, navigating blind or dark environments.

### Air / Positive
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — mental and sensory restoration
**Expression:** Restorative wind clearing corruption from mind and senses. Air carrying Positive energy cleanses mental conditions, clears confusion, and restores sensory clarity. The breath of life applied inward.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Suppress one mental [Hindered] condition (Dazed, Disoriented, Frightened) for duration | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | Remove one mental [Hindered] condition entirely, suppress a second for duration | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | Remove all current mental [Hindered] conditions, immunity to new mental conditions for duration | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Non-Combat:** At 5+ cost, removes Sickened condition without a Medicine check. At 7 cost, clears early-stage disease symptoms for the duration — does not cure the disease but suppresses active symptoms.
**Tell:** Visibly cleaner breath. Eyes clear. Posture straightens as conditions lift.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Strong non-combat application for disease symptom management and recovery from mental conditions.

### Air / Negative
**Category:** Presence
**Table:** Presence — internal roll as save target
**Expression:** Air carrying Negative energy radiates outward as suffocating entropy. Not a weave — the body becomes a source of life-draining absence. Nearby creatures feel vitality being pulled toward the character. The character does not project this deliberately; they *become* the effect.

This is a pure Presence effect with no Expression or Enhancement component. The effect radiates passively for the duration. Always triggers an Exhaustion check regardless of energy spent.

Creatures within range make a Fortitude save against the internal roll result each round they remain in range.

| Primary Cost | Range | Effect on Failed Save | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|-------|----------------------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | 10 feet | Fatigued (1 Exhaustion level) — save each round | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | 15 feet | Fatigued + Weakened — save each round | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | 20 feet | Exhausted (2 Exhaustion levels) + Weakened — save each round | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Exhaustion:** Always triggers Exhaustion check. The character is sustaining entropy through their own body as a passive field.
**Tell:** Air near the character feels thin. Torches and flames within range dim and waver. Animals become distressed and avoid the area.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Extended non-combat use corrupts an area perceptibly — useful for clearing spaces, intimidation, or creating locations others instinctively avoid.

### Air / Space
**Category:** Adaptation
**Table:** Transformation table — sensory grants
**Expression:** Sound crossing impossible distances, vibration perceived through space beyond normal range. The character's hearing becomes qualitatively different — not just louder, but capable of processing information normal hearing cannot reach.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Hearing range doubled, cannot be Deafened by non-magical sources | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Hearing range 60 feet through solid surfaces, Deafened immunity, detect hidden creatures by sound within 30 feet | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + tremorsense 30 feet (detect through ground vibration), cannot be Surprised while active | 4 — 1 hour |

**Tell:** Head tilts and orients to sounds others cannot hear. Slight echo quality to the character's own voice during the effect.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Strong non-combat utility for eavesdropping, navigation in darkness, detection of approaching threats through walls or at distance.

---

### Air / Time
**Category:** Enhancement
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — action precision
**Expression:** Sustained resonance — Air and Time produce a state where the character's actions find their natural rhythm and maintain it. Less explosive than Air/Fire, this is enduring precision rather than reactive speed. The fighter who has been in motion for hours and is somehow more accurate at the end than the beginning. The surgeon whose hands do not shake after three hours at the table.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Subtract 1 from skill die on all Dexterity-based checks | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Subtract 1 from both dice on all Dexterity-based checks | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + reduce third Combat action Multiple Action Penalty from +2 to +1 | 4 — 1 hour |

**Tell:** Movement becomes rhythmic and deliberate. Breathing audibly paced. The character seems to be working to a rhythm others cannot hear.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. One of the strongest non-combat Air pairings — sustained precision applies directly to Craft checks, Medicine, performance, and any extended Dexterity-based task during downtime. A character with Air/Time active during crafting work may claim a bonus at GM discretion.
