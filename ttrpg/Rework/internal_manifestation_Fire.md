# Fire — Internal Manifestation

## Overview

Fire internally is hunger, intensity, and consuming focus. It burns through hesitation, exhaustion, and physical limitation. The risk mirrors external Fire — it can break free from control, consuming the user as readily as anything else. Characters channeling Fire internally push beyond normal limits, but the body pays a price.

---

## Pairings

---

### Fire / Air
**Category:** Enhancement
**Table:** Buff/Debuff
**Expression:** Explosive burst of speed and momentum. Fire fed by Air produces a single dramatic movement or strike enhancement — a charge, a repositioning, a burst of combat speed. Cannot be sustained; Fire burns fast when Air feeds it.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 Agility for one action or movement | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 Agility for one round | 1 — 1 round |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 Agility + one additional Move action | 2 — 1 minute |

**Tell:** Heat shimmer around the body. Hair and clothing move in a sourceless wind. Breath visible despite the heat.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Primarily combat. Useful for chase and escape.

### Fire / Earth
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — Exhaustion interaction
**Expression:** Sustained burning endurance. Fire consumes the sensation of fatigue, allowing the character to perform beyond normal physical limits. Does not remove Exhaustion — suppresses its mechanical penalties for the duration. When the effect ends, full Exhaustion penalties return immediately. The damage and the Exhaustion are still there. The Fire simply makes the character unable to feel them.

Mechanically, suppression means: treat current Exhaustion level as one step lower for all mechanical purposes during the duration.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Treat current Exhaustion as one level lower | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | Treat current Exhaustion as two levels lower | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | Ignore all Exhaustion penalties + no Exhaustion check this scene for this use only | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Note:** At 7 cost, the Exhaustion check exemption applies only to this specific activation. Other internal manifestation used the same scene still triggers checks normally.

**Tell:** Skin radiates visible heat. Eyes dry and reddened. No sweat despite exertion.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Strong non-combat application for forced march, extreme environmental survival, extended physical labor.

### Fire / Water
**Category:** Expression
**Table:** Expression sub-table
**Expression:** Attacks carry scalding thermal energy. Heat transfers through the point of contact as steam and thermal shock — working through armor gaps and joints rather than burning the surface. Life-leach variant available at higher Mastery investment.

| Primary Cost | Bonus Damage | Condition | Life-Leach Variant |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 fire | Singed on margin 2+ | 1 HP returned |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 fire | Smoldering on margin 2+ | 2 HP returned |
| 7 (6+1) | +8 fire | Ignited on margin 2+ | 4 HP returned |

**DR:** Energy damage, half DR.
**Duration:** Instantaneous per strike. To sustain the effect across multiple strikes, invest in Secondary duration using the duration table.
**Tell:** Hands and striking surfaces visibly hot. Weapons held for extended periods show heat stress.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Combat.

### Fire / Positive
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff for combat suppression. Existing disease and poison recovery rules apply outside combat with reduced timeframes.
**Expression:** Fire burns away corruption. In combat, suppresses one active negative condition per round. Outside combat, the purifying heat accelerates recovery from disease and poison — the body burns through what would otherwise take days.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Suppress one minor [Hindered] condition per round | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | Suppress one moderate [Hindered] condition per round | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | Suppress one major [Hindered] condition, or two minor conditions | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Non-Combat:** At 5+ primary cost, reduces disease or poison severity by one stage per long rest without requiring a Medicine check.
**Tell:** Fever warmth. Skin flushed. Faint persistent smell of ash even when clean.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. One of the stronger non-combat Fire pairings.

### Fire / Negative
**Category:** Expression with Presence at higher investment
**Table:** Expression sub-table. Presence uses internal roll as save target.
**Expression:** Consuming fire fueled by entropic sacrifice. Strikes deal fire damage and the character pays an HP cost to activate — burning their own vitality as fuel. More potent than Fire/Water because the cost is real and non-recoverable until rest.

The HP cost buys one tier upgrade on the Expression table without additional energy expenditure. You are trading recoverable HP for energy efficiency.

At 7+ primary cost with Negative Mastery 4+, Presence activates: nearby creatures within 20 feet feel the consuming hunger. Will save against the internal roll result or gain the Frightened condition (Level 1 Fear).

| Primary Cost | Standard Effect | With HP Cost (4 HP) | Presence (7+ only) |
|-------------|----------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 fire, Singed on margin 2+ | Performs as 5 cost | — |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 fire, Smoldering on margin 2+ | Performs as 7 cost | — |
| 7 (6+1) | +8 fire, Ignited on margin 2+ | Performs as 7 cost + Presence | Frightened (Will save vs internal roll) |

**DR:** Energy damage, half DR.
**HP Cost:** Flat 4 HP per activation. Not scaling. Represents deliberate self-sacrifice.
**Exhaustion:** Fire/Negative always triggers an Exhaustion check regardless of energy spent, due to the HP cost component.
**Tell:** Eyes darken at the edges. Visible heat but cold to the touch. Nearby flames burn lower, as if competing for fuel.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Primarily combat. HP cost gives narrative weight to non-combat use as deliberate sacrifice.

### Fire / Space
**Category:** Adaptation
**Table:** Transformation table for capability grants. Buff/Debuff for the sensing component.
**Expression:** Two distinct effects available at different investment levels, reflecting the different natures of Fire and Space in combination.

**Heat Sensing:** The character perceives living bodies by their heat signature through obstacles — warm targets through walls, in darkness, through concealment. Aware of location but not detail.

**Blink Movement:** The character crosses short distances faster than physics allows, leaving a brief heat trail at the point of origin. This is not teleportation — the body moves, it simply moves faster than it should. Does not provoke opportunity attacks on the movement itself.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Heat sensing 30 feet | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Heat sensing 60 feet + blink up to 15 feet once per activation | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | Heat sensing 60 feet + blink up to 30 feet, usable once per round | 4 — 1 hour |

**Tell:** Movement leaves faint scorch marks at origin points. Heat afterimage briefly visible where the character stood.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Heat sensing is strong non-combat utility for tracking, navigation in darkness, and detecting ambush through walls or concealment.

### Fire / Time
**Category:** Enhancement
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — Multiple Action Penalty interaction
**Expression:** Fire burns at a controlled rate across time rather than spiking. Sustained combat enhancement — the character's intensity is maintained across a long engagement without degrading. The last blow of a long fight hits as hard as the first. Unlike Fire/Air which produces a single explosive burst, Fire/Time is the long burn that doesn't fade.

Outside combat, this pairing sustains precision across extended tasks that would normally degrade with fatigue — forging, surgery, intricate craft, demanding performance.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Reduce Multiple Action Penalty by 1 on all Combat actions | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Reduce Multiple Action Penalty by 2 on all Combat actions | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | Eliminate Multiple Action Penalty entirely on all Combat actions | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Exhaustion note:** The 7 cost tier always triggers an Exhaustion check regardless of total energy spent that scene. Eliminating the Multiple Action Penalty across a full encounter is the body doing something it genuinely should not sustain. The fire is burning time itself as fuel.

**Tell:** Steady, even heat throughout the duration with no fluctuation. Unnerving steadiness of expression and movement rhythm — the character does not visibly tire.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Combat primarily. Extended precision tasks have meaningful non-combat application.
