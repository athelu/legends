# Diseases

## Filth Fever
**Severity:** [Mild]
**Vector:** Wounds from rats, refuse, contaminated water
**Incubation:** 1d8 days
**Recovery:** Fortitude save at end of each long rest (Easy task — 1 success cures).
**Effects:**
- **Stage 1:** Sickened condition. Fortitude saves: add 1 to die results.
- **Stage 2:** Sickened condition + Fatigued (Exhaustion Level 1). Constitution-based skill checks: add 1 to die results.
- **Stage 3:** As Stage 2. Failure at this stage deals 4 damage per long rest until cured.

## Rotting Plague
**Severity:** [Serious]
**Vector:** Undead claws, corpse-handling without protection, necrotic wounds
**Incubation:** 2d8 days
**Recovery:** Fortitude save at end of each long rest (Hard task — 2 successes cures; 1 success = no change; 0 = worsen).
**Effects:**
- **Stage 1:** Weakened condition. Strength-based checks: add 1 to die results.
- **Stage 2:** Weakened condition + Sickened condition. Strength and Constitution checks: add 1 to die results each.
- **Stage 3:** As Stage 2. Wounds do not close — the character cannot benefit from natural HP recovery during short or long rests (magical healing still works).
- **Stage 4:** As Stage 3. Failure at this stage deals 8 damage per long rest until cured.

## Demon Fever
**Severity:** [Deadly]
**Vector:** Daemon claws or bites, nexus-adjacent contamination, exposure to corrupted pneuma
**Incubation:** 1d8 days
**Recovery:** Fortitude save at end of each long rest (Hard task — 2 successes cures; 1 success = no change; 0 = worsen). Magical healing alone suppresses symptoms for 24 hours but does not cure; Major Restoration cures completely.
**Active Effects:**
- **Stage 1:** Disoriented condition. Intelligence and Wisdom checks: add 1 to both die results. The character's eyes occasionally flicker with unnatural light.
- **Stage 2:** Disoriented condition + Dazed (once per long rest, the character experiences an episode lasting 1 hour where they are Dazed and cannot be reasoned with).
- **Stage 3:** As Stage 2. The character cannot benefit from long rests — their pneuma is too disrupted to settle. Luck does not restore on rest.
- **Stage 4:** As Stage 3. Failure at this stage deals 12 damage per long rest and the character makes a Will save (Hard task) or acts erratically for 1d8 hours (GM determines behavior). Death is possible if untreated.
