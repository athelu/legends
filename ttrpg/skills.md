### Skills System

#### Core Skills (24 skills tied to attributes)

##### Strength (2 skills)
-   Athletics
-   Might (breaking, lifting, raw power)

##### Dexterity (5 skills)
-   Devices (locks, traps, mechanisms)
-   Thievery (pickpocketing, sleight of hand)
-   Writing (calligraphy, forgery)
-   Ranged Combat
-   Craft

##### Agility (3 skills)
-   Acrobatics
-   Melee Combat
-   Stealth

##### Intelligence (5 skills)
-   Investigate
-   Language (deciphering unknown languages; rank = number of languages known)
-   History
-   Arcane (magical knowledge, identifying magic)
-   Society

##### Wisdom (5 skills)
-   Perception
-   Empathy
-   Medicine
-   Wilderness (nature and survival combined)
-   Religion

##### Charisma (4 skills)
-   Persuasion
-   Intimidate
-   Perform
-   Deception

##### Constitution - No skills, used for HP calculation and Fortitude saves

##### Luck - No skills, used for Luck pool and all saving throws
