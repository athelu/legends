# D8 TTRPG - Part 4: Traits

## TRAITS (Cost Points)

### Acute Hearing (-2)
Ranges of sound increased.
-   Initiative: +1
-   Sound-based Perception: Subtract 1 from die results

### Acute Taste and Smell (-2)
Beyond normal capabilities.
-   Can detect poison with Perception check
-   Perception using these senses: Subtract 1 from die results
-   Can track by scent

### Alertness (-3)
Aware of surroundings.
-   Initiative: +1
-   All Perception: Subtract 1 from die results

### Bardic Magic (-5)
**Requirements:** Charisma ≥ 3 Music and performance as magical conduit.
-   Roll **4d8**, assign to four Magical Potentials only
-   **Air Potential** = highest roll + 2 (max 8) [always primary - music, sound, voice]
-   Choose **Performance Focus** (Positive, Space, or Time)
-   That Potential = any other roll + 1
-   Assign remaining two rolls to any two other energies
-   Use **Charisma** as Casting Stat
-   Gain only **4 Magical Mastery skills** corresponding to your 4 chosen energies (start at rank 0, untrained)
-   Can weave bardic magic using only those 4 energies
-   **Energy Pool:** Calculate using only your 4 Potentials + (Charisma × 2) + Con + (Total Mastery ÷ 2)
-   **Gain Inspiring Performance ability:** Once per short rest, spend 1 [Combat] action to perform. All allies within 30 feet subtract 1 from both dice on their next roll.

### Contacts (-1 to -3)
Associates or influential people for information/favors.
-   One Contact (-1)
-   Two Contacts (-2)
-   Three Contacts (-3)

### Double-Jointed (-3)
Hyper-extend joints.
-   Acrobatics starts at rank 3 at creation
-   Reflex saves: Subtract 1 from die results

### Eidetic Memory (-5)
Remember everything seen/heard.
-   Intelligence check to recall any encountered information
-   History, Society, Arcane: Subtract 2 from die results

### Good Luck (-3)
Luck runs high.
-   Once per session, reroll any single die, take better result

### Highly Skilled (-5)
Excellent education in focus area.
-   Choose one skill to start at rank 4 at creation
-   That skill's governing attribute: +1

### Immortality (-10)
Eternal lifespan.
-   Don't die of natural causes/disease
-   Age to race's prime and stop
-   Can be killed by violence/magic
-   **Requires GM approval**

### Intuition (-3)
Overdeveloped sixth sense.
-   Perception and Investigate: Subtract 1 from die results
-   GM may give "hunches"

### Keen Sight (-2)
Vision better than normal.
-   Vision-based Perception: Subtract 1 from die results

### Light Sleeper (-2)
Subtle noises awaken easily.
-   Not surprised while sleeping
-   No penalty when awakened

### Lightning Reflexes (-5)
Unparalleled quickness.
-   Initiative: +2
-   All Perception: Subtract 1 from die results
-   Reflex saves: Subtract 1 from die results

### Lion Heart (-3)
Unfaltering courage.
-   Will saves: Subtract 1 from die results
-   Subtract 1 from both fear save dice

### Longevity (-5)
Longer lifespan.
-   Double race\'s maximum age

### Loyalty (-1)
Can be trusted.
-   Persuasion when reputation known: Subtract 1 from die results

### Luckiness (-2)
Fortune favors you.
-   Start each session with 2 bonus Luck points beyond attribute

### Magical Aptitude (-7)
**Requirements:** Intelligence ≥ 3 Studied arcane weaving.
-   Roll 8d8, assign to Magical Potentials
-   Choose **Elemental Affinity** (Earth/Air/Fire/Water): That Potential = highest roll + 2 (max 8)
-   Choose **Secondary Focus** (any other energy): That Potential = any other roll + 1
-   Use **Intelligence** as Casting Stat
-   Gain 8 Magical Mastery skills (start at rank 0, untrained)
-   Can weave arcane magic

-   **Gain Ritual Casting ability:** Can cast any weave as a 10-minute ritual for half Energy cost (minimum 1). Cannot be used in combat.

### Divine Gift (-7)
**Requirements:** Wisdom ≥ 3 Blessed by divine forces.
-   Roll 8d8, assign to Magical Potentials
-   **Choose Divine Path:**
    -   **Cleric Path:** Choose Divine Affinity (Positive or Negative).
        Chosen affinity = highest roll + 2 (max 8), other divine energy
        = any other roll + 1

    -   **Druid Path:** Choose Elemental Affinity (Earth, Air, Fire, or
        Water) = highest roll + 2 (max 8), and choose one divine energy
        (Positive or Negative) = any other roll + 1
-   Use **Wisdom** as Casting Stat
-   Gain 8 Magical Mastery skills (start at rank 0, untrained)
-   Can weave divine magic
-   **Gain Channel Divinity ability**

### Gifted Mage (-2)
**Requirements:** Must have Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift Enhanced
magical talent.
-   Roll 9d8, drop lowest
-   Advantage bonuses still apply

### Balanced Channeler (-1)
**Requirements:** Must have Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift Predictable magical talent.
-   Use fixed array: 5,4,3,3,2,2,1,1
-   Advantage bonuses still apply

### Mentor (-10)
Youth mentor trained you extensively.
-   +40 XP at character creation
-   Choose one skill to start at rank 5

-   **Requires GM approval and backstory**

### Mythic Characteristic (-10)
Extraordinary ability like legendary heroes.
-   One attribute: +2 (cannot exceed 8)
-   **May only be taken once**
-   **Requires GM approval**

### Touch of Good (-3)
From surprisingly good family stock.
-   Persuasion: Subtract 1 from die results
-   NPCs start with favorable impression

### Wisdom (-5)
Blessed with insight.
-   May attempt Perception/Intelligence knowledge checks when untrained
-   Once per session, ask GM for hint/insight
