# D8 TTRPG - Part 4: Traits

## TRAITS (Cost Points)

### Acute Hearing (-2)
Ranges of sound increased.
-   Initiative: +1
-   Sound-based Perception: Subtract 1 from die results

### Acute Taste and Smell (-2)
Beyond normal capabilities.
-   Can detect poison with Perception check
-   Perception using these senses: Subtract 1 from die results
-   Can track by scent

### Alertness (-3)
Aware of surroundings.
-   Initiative: +1
-   All Perception: Subtract 1 from die results


### Balanced Channeler (-1)
**Requirements:** Must have Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift Predictable magical talent.
-   Use fixed array: 5,4,3,3,2,2,1,1
-   Advantage bonuses still apply

### Bardic Magic (-5)
**Requirements:** Charisma ≥ 3 Music and performance as magical conduit.
-   Roll **5d8**, assign to five Magical Potentials:
-   **Air Potential** = highest roll + 2 (max 8) [always primary - music, sound, voice]
-   **Positive Potential** = second-highest roll + 1 [healing and inspiration]
-   **Space Potential** = any remaining roll [illusions, range, positioning]
-   **Time Potential** = any remaining roll [duration, temporal effects]
-   **Choose one Elemental or Conceptual energy** (Earth, Fire, Water, or Negative): assign final roll
-   Use **Charisma** as Casting Stat
-   Gain **5 Magical Mastery skills** corresponding to your 5 energies (start at rank 0, untrained)
-   Can weave bardic magic using only these 5 energies
-   **Energy Pool:** Sum of 5 Potentials + (Charisma × 2) + Con + (Total Mastery ÷ 2)
-   **Gain Inspiring Performance ability:** Once per short rest, spend 1 [Combat] action to perform. All allies within 30 feet subtract 1 from both dice on their next roll.

**Elemental Energy Themes:**
-   **Fire**: Flashy performances, fire-breathers, passionate artistry
-   **Water**: Graceful dancers, ice sculptors, flowing movements  
-   **Earth**: Drum circles, nature singers, stone resonance, stomping rhythms
-   **Negative**: Dirge singers, horror storytellers, theatrical darkness, requiems

### Contacts (-1 to -3)
Associates or influential people for information/favors.
-   One Contact (-1)
-   Two Contacts (-2)
-   Three Contacts (-3)

### Divine Gift (-7)
**Requirements:** Wisdom ≥ 3 Blessed by divine forces.
-   Roll 8d8, assign to Magical Potentials
-   **Choose Divine Path:**
    -   **Cleric Path:** Choose Divine Affinity (Positive or Negative).
        Chosen affinity = highest roll + 2 (max 8), other divine energy
        = any other roll + 1

    -   **Druid Path:** Choose Elemental Affinity (Earth, Air, Fire, or
        Water) = highest roll + 2 (max 8), and choose one divine energy
        (Positive or Negative) = any other roll + 1
-   Use **Wisdom** as Casting Stat
-   Gain 8 Magical Mastery skills (start at rank 0, untrained)
-   Can weave divine magic
-   **Gain Channel Divinity ability**

### Divine Warrior (-5)
**Requirements:** Wisdom ≥ 3, Charisma ≥ 3

Choose **Sacred Oath:**
- **Devotion:** +1 Charisma, +1 Wisdom (protection, healing)
- **Vengeance:** +1 Strength, +1 Charisma (smiting, justice)
- **Ancients:** +1 Wisdom, +1 Constitution (nature, warding)
- **Conquest:** +1 Strength, +1 Charisma (domination, fear)

**Limited Divine Weaving:**
- Roll **4d8**, assign to four Magical Potentials only:
  - **Positive Potential** = highest roll + 2 (max 8) [always primary - healing, protection, radiance]
  - **Choose Secondary Focus** (Space or Time): That Potential = second-highest roll + 1
  - Assign remaining two rolls to the other two energies (Space/Time + one choice: Earth, Fire, Water, or Negative)
- Use **Charisma** as Casting Stat
- Gain **4 Magical Mastery skills** corresponding to your 4 energies (start rank 0)
- Can weave divine magic using only these 4 energies
- **Energy Pool:** Sum of 4 Potentials + (Charisma × 2) + Con + (Total Mastery ÷ 2)
- Energy regenerates on **short rest** (unlike full casters who need long rest)

**Gain Lay on Hands:** You have a healing pool equal to Charisma × 5. As 1 [Combat] action, touch a creature and restore any amount from your pool. Pool restores after long rest.

**Unlocks Access To:** Paladin feat trees

### Double-Jointed (-3)
Hyper-extend joints.
-   Acrobatics starts at rank 3 at creation
-   Reflex saves: Subtract 1 from die results

### Eidetic Memory (-5)
Remember everything seen/heard.
-   Intelligence check to recall any encountered information
-   History, Society, Arcane: Subtract 2 from die results

### Gifted Mage (-2)
**Requirements:** Must have Magical Aptitude or Divine Gift Enhanced
magical talent.
-   Roll 9d8, drop lowest
-   Advantage bonuses still apply

### Good Luck (-3)
Luck runs high.
-   Once per session, reroll any single die, take better result

### Highly Skilled (-5)
Excellent education in focus area.
-   Choose one skill to start at rank 4 at creation
-   That skill's governing attribute: +1

### Immortality (-10)
Eternal lifespan.
-   Don't die of natural causes/disease
-   Age to race's prime and stop
-   Can be killed by violence/magic
-   **Requires GM approval**

### Intuition (-3)
Overdeveloped sixth sense.
-   Perception and Investigate: Subtract 1 from die results
-   GM may give "hunches"

### Keen Sight (-2)
Vision better than normal.
-   Vision-based Perception: Subtract 1 from die results

### Light Sleeper (-2)
Subtle noises awaken easily.
-   Not surprised while sleeping
-   No penalty when awakened

### Lightning Reflexes (-5)
Unparalleled quickness.
-   Initiative: +2
-   All Perception: Subtract 1 from die results
-   Reflex saves: Subtract 1 from die results

### Lion Heart (-3)
Unfaltering courage.
-   Will saves: Subtract 1 from die results
-   Subtract 1 from both fear save dice

### Longevity (-5)
Longer lifespan.
-   Double race\'s maximum age

### Loyalty (-1)
Can be trusted.
-   Persuasion when reputation known: Subtract 1 from die results

### Luckiness (-2)
Fortune favors you.
-   Start each session with 2 bonus Luck points beyond attribute

### Magical Aptitude (-7)
**Requirements:** Intelligence ≥ 3 Studied arcane weaving.
-   Roll 8d8, assign to Magical Potentials
-   Choose **Elemental Affinity** (Earth/Air/Fire/Water): That Potential = highest roll + 2 (max 8)
-   Choose **Secondary Focus** (any other energy): That Potential = any other roll + 1
-   Use **Intelligence** as Casting Stat
-   Gain 8 Magical Mastery skills (start at rank 0, untrained)
-   Can weave arcane magic

-   **Gain Ritual Casting ability:** Can cast any weave as a 10-minute ritual for half Energy cost (minimum 1). Cannot be used in combat.

### Martial Prowess (-5)
**Requirements:** Strength or Agility ≥ 3

Choose **Martial Style:**
- **Berserker:** +1 Strength, +1 Constitution. While wearing no armor, gain DR equal to half your Constitution (rounded down).
- **Weapon Master:** +2 to chosen combat attribute (Strength, Agility, or Dexterity)
- **Defender:** +1 Constitution, +1 to choice of Strength or Agility
- **Skirmisher:** +1 Agility, +1 Dexterity

**Gain Martial Training:** Reduce multiple attack penalty by 1 for all attacks after the first.

**Unlocks Access To:** Martial feat trees

### Mentor (-10)
Youth mentor trained you extensively.
-   +40 XP at character creation
-   Choose one skill to start at rank 5

-   **Requires GM approval and backstory**

### Monastic Training (-5)
**Requirements:** Agility ≥ 3, Wisdom ≥ 3

Choose **Monastic Tradition:**
- **Open Hand:** +1 Agility, +1 Strength
- **Shadow:** +1 Agility, +1 Wisdom
- **Four Elements:** +1 Wisdom, +1 Constitution
- **Drunken Master:** +1 Agility, +1 Constitution

**Gain Unarmed Mastery:**
- Unarmed strikes count as monk weapons
- Unarmed strikes deal 6 damage (same as standard weapon)

**Gain Unarmored Defense:**
- While wearing no armor and not using a shield, gain DR equal to your Wisdom score

**Gain Flurry of Blows:** Once per turn, when you make an unarmed attack or monk weapon attack, you may make three strikes as a single [Combat] action:
- **Use Shared Attribute Roll:** Roll your Agility die once, use that result for all three attacks
- **Roll Melee Combat skill die separately** for each attack
- **Apply penalties to skill die only:**
  - First attack: Normal
  - Second attack: Add 1 to skill die
  - Third attack: Add 2 to skill die
- All three attacks count as one [Combat] action for multiple action penalty purposes

**Unlocks Access To:** Monk feat trees

### Mythic Characteristic (-10)
Extraordinary ability like legendary heroes.
-   One attribute: +2 (cannot exceed 8)
-   **May only be taken once**
-   **Requires GM approval**

### Ranger's Path (-5)
**Requirements:** Wisdom ≥ 3

Choose **Ranger Focus:**
- **Beast Master:** +1 Wisdom, +1 Charisma. Gain animal companion (use Conjure Animals rules, 1/day for 1 hour, no Energy cost)
- **Hunter:** +1 Dexterity, +1 Wisdom
- **Scout:** +1 Agility, +1 Wisdom
- **Horizon Walker:** +1 Agility, +1 Wisdom

**Gain Favored Enemy:** Choose creature type. Subtract 1 from both dice when tracking or fighting that type.

**Unlocks Access To:** Ranger feat trees

### Roguish Training (-5)
**Requirements:** Agility ≥ 3

Choose **Rogue Archetype:**
- **Assassin:** +1 Agility, +1 Dexterity
- **Thief:** +1 Agility, +1 Wisdom
- **Trickster:** +1 Agility, +1 Charisma
- **Scout:** +1 Agility, +1 Wisdom

**Gain Sneak Attack:** Once per round, when you attack with Fortune OR an ally is within 5 feet of target, add your Dexterity score to damage.

**Unlocks Access To:** Roguish feat trees

### Touch of Good (-3)
From surprisingly good family stock.
-   Persuasion: Subtract 1 from die results
-   NPCs start with favorable impression

### Wisdom (-5)
Blessed with insight.
-   May attempt Perception/Intelligence knowledge checks when untrained
-   Once per session, ask GM for hint/insight
