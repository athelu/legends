# Earth — Internal Manifestation

## Overview

The Earth energy internal manifestation is the most physical of all energies. The character channeling Earth internally becomes harder, heavier and more permanent. Earth has the strongest physical Resilience expressions of any primary energy, the best DR bonuses, and the most reliable non-combat utility through material interaction.

---
