# Water — Internal Manifestation

## Overview

Water internally is the engine of change, adaptability, and persistence. Where Earth resists and Fire consumes, Water finds the path of least resistance — it does not oppose force, it redirects it. A character channeling Water internally becomes fluid in response, absorbing impacts, adapting to environments, and persisting through conditions that would break a more rigid approach.

Water has the strongest environmental adaptation expressions, the best condition resistance against physical and chemical threats, and significant social utility — Water reads undercurrents in people the way it reads terrain. Its weakest area is direct combat offense; Water primary does not hit hard, it survives and redirects.

## Pairings

---

### Water / Fire
**Category:** Adaptation
**Table:** Transformation table — thermal regulation
**Expression:** Water carrying Fire internally produces extraordinary thermal regulation. The character's body manages heat and cold simultaneously — not immunity, but a profound ability to maintain equilibrium against thermal extremes in either direction. Steam and thermal balance rather than burning or freezing.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Ignore environmental heat and cold penalties up to moderate severity | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Ignore all environmental thermal conditions, +2 DR against fire and cold energy damage | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + immune to Singed and Frosted conditions, +4 DR against fire and cold energy damage | 4 — 1 hour |

**Tell:** Skin temperature seems inconsistent to the touch — warm in cold environments, cool in hot ones. Breath regulation is visibly unusual. The character neither sweats nor shivers in conditions that should cause both.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Strong environmental utility for extreme climate survival, forced march through desert or arctic conditions, navigating burning structures.

### Water / Air
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — force absorption and redirection
**Expression:** Water and Air together produce the body's ability to absorb and redirect physical force — the same way ice forms from water given direction by air. The character does not resist hits, they flow around them, reducing impact and recovering position.

At 7 cost, force redirection activates: once per round when hit by a melee physical attack, 4 damage returns to the attacker as bludgeoning. This is not a counter-attack — it is force returning through contact. It requires no action and is not subject to Multiple Action Penalty.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Reduce all physical damage taken by 2, cannot be knocked Prone by physical impacts | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | Reduce all physical damage taken by 4, cannot be knocked Prone, reduce forced movement by 10 feet | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + once per round, redirect 4 damage from a melee physical hit back to attacker as bludgeoning | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Tell:** Movement takes on a flowing, circular quality. Hits seem to land at angles rather than flush. The character recovers position from impacts faster than physics suggests they should.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Combat primarily. Force absorption has minor utility against environmental physical hazards.

### Water / Earth
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — poison and disease resistance
**Expression:** Flood carrying mass — Water grounded through Earth produces the body's most robust chemical and biological resistance. The body becomes a saturated system that contaminants struggle to penetrate. Where Fire/Positive burns corruption out, Water/Earth simply does not let it take hold.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 to Fortitude saves against poison and disease | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Immunity to Weak poison, +4 to Fortitude saves against Strong and Deadly poison | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | Immunity to Weak and Strong poison, +4 Fortitude saves against Deadly poison, Mild diseases cannot take hold | 4 — 1 hour |

**Non-Combat:** At 5+ cost, actively suppresses active Weak poison without a Medicine check. At 7 cost, reduces an active Strong poison by one severity level at the start of each hour without external treatment.
**Tell:** Skin has a faintly damp quality regardless of conditions. The character's constitution seems indifferent to food and drink quality — they consume things that would sicken others without apparent effect.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. One of the strongest non-combat Water pairings for environments with endemic disease or poison threats.

### Water / Positive
**Category:** Resilience
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — persistent recovery
**Expression:** Cleansing water with Positive energy produces deep, persistent healing that builds over time rather than restoring in a single burst. The body's own recovery processes are amplified — wounds close, fatigue clears, the system resets. This is the most healing-adjacent internal manifestation available to any character.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Regain 1 HP at the start of each of your turns | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Regain 2 HP at the start of each turn, suppress one Bleeding stack per round | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | Regain 3 HP at start of each turn, immune to Bleeding condition, +2 to Fortitude saves | 4 — 1 hour |

**Non-Combat:** At any investment level, activating Water/Positive during a short rest adds the HP recovery per minute to the rest's total recovery. At 7 cost, reduces one Exhaustion level during a short rest rather than requiring a long rest.
**Tell:** Minor wounds visibly close during the duration. Skin takes on a faint luminescent quality at the surface. The character seems less affected by exertion as the effect persists.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. The strongest sustained non-combat recovery pairing in the Water primary set.

### Water / Negative
**Category:** Adaptation with Presence at higher investment
**Table:** Transformation table for primary effect. Presence uses internal roll as save target.
**Expression:** Corruption carried in liquid — Water channeling Negative energy produces the body's ability to process and carry toxicity without being harmed by it. The character becomes a controlled vector for poison and disease, immune to their own biological output. At higher investment this extends outward: proximity to the character becomes unhealthy for others.

Touch delivery requires a successful unarmed strike or grapple — the target must make skin contact with the character. Gloves and similar barriers prevent delivery.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Immune to own poison and disease effects, touch delivers Weak poison (Fortitude save vs internal roll) | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | As above + touch delivers Strong poison, immune to Weak and Strong poisons from external sources | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | As above + Presence: creatures within 20 feet make Fortitude save each round vs internal roll or gain Sickened condition | 4 — 1 hour |

**Exhaustion:** Always triggers Exhaustion check when Presence is active.
**Tell:** Faint discoloration at fingertips and lips. The character's breath has a medicinal or chemical quality. Small plants wilt after extended proximity.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Non-combat applications include contaminating water sources, delivering effects through physical contact, and creating areas of sustained biological hazard.

### Water / Space
**Category:** Adaptation
**Table:** Transformation table — fluid spatial awareness
**Expression:** Water finding the path of least resistance through space. The character develops an extraordinary sense of their physical environment — knowing the layout of spaces by feel, sensing the location of obstacles and openings, navigating without sight. At higher investment, surface emotional states of nearby creatures become perceptible — Water reads the undercurrents in people the same way it reads terrain.

The emotional sensing at 7 cost is not mind-reading. It perceives broad emotional states — fear, anger, deception, calm — in the same way water perceives the shape of its container. It gives no access to thoughts or specifics.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Blindsense 15 feet — aware of all creatures and objects within range regardless of visibility | 2 — 1 minute |
| 5 (4+1) | Blindsense 30 feet, immune to Blinded condition for duration | 3 — 10 minutes |
| 7 (6+1) | Blindsense 60 feet, immune to Blinded, sense surface emotional state of creatures within 30 feet | 4 — 1 hour |

**Tell:** Eyes lose focus during activation — the character is no longer relying on them. Head orients toward sounds and movement rather than visual cues. Movement through crowded spaces becomes uncannily smooth.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Both. Blindsense is strong in darkness, fog, or against invisible opponents. Emotional sensing is one of the most significant social utility effects in the system — a character with Water/Space active during negotiation or interrogation perceives deception and fear directly.

### Water / Time
**Category:** Enhancement
**Table:** Buff/Debuff — persistent erosion and endurance
**Expression:** Erosion compressed into moments — Water and Time together produce relentless, wearing pressure. The character does not hit harder; they wear opponents down, and they themselves become harder to wear down. The character who has been fighting for an hour becomes more dangerous than the character who just started, because Water/Time accumulates rather than sustains.

The DR reduction from strikes stacks with each hit and persists for the scene regardless of whether the effect's duration expires. The erosion has already happened. Activating Water/Time and letting it expire does not reset the accumulated DR reduction on affected targets.

The energy regeneration at 7 cost is the only internal manifestation effect that returns energy mid-scene. This is intentional — Water/Time is the long fight pairing, the attrition specialist.

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | Attacks that hit reduce target's DR by 1 (cumulative, max reduction 3) until end of scene | 1 — 1 round |
| 5 (4+1) | As above (max reduction 4) + character ignores first Exhaustion level gained this scene | 2 — 1 minute |
| 7 (6+1) | As above (max reduction 5) + at the start of each round regain 1 energy point (cannot exceed pool maximum) | 3 — 10 minutes |

**Tell:** Movement becomes rhythmic and tidal — advancing and withdrawing with a quality of inevitability. Strikes leave no visible mark but opponents begin to slow and tire in ways that seem disproportionate to the damage taken.
**Combat/Non-Combat:** Combat primarily. Endurance extension has non-combat application for sustained physical labor and extended activity.
