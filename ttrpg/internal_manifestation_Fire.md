# Fire — Internal Manifestation

## Overview

The internal manifestation of Fire is hunger, intensity, and an all consuming focus. It burns through hesitation, exhaustion, and physical limitation.

---

## Pairings

### Fire / Air
**Category:** Enhancement
**Table:** Buff/Debuff
**Tell:** Heat shimmer around the body. 
**Expression:** Explosive burst of speed and momentum. 
**Exhaustion:** no

| Primary Cost | Effect | Secondary Duration Cost |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 Agility for one action or movement | 0 — instantaneous |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 Agility for one round | 1 — 1 round |
| 7 (6+1) | +4 Agility + one additional Move action | 2 — 1 minute |
