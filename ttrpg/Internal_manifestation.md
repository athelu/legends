## Core Mechanics

### Simple Internal Manifestation
- **Cost:** 1 action, no Multiple Action Penalty
- **Energy:** Primary only, instantaneous
- **Roll:** None required
- **Effect:** Spend energy, effect happens immediately

### Complex Internal Manifestation
- **Cost:** 1 action, no Multiple Action Penalty
- **Energy:** Primary + Secondary (Secondary covers duration only — range and area costs do not apply)
- **Primary cost:** +1 above equivalent external effect at same magnitude
- **Roll:** Primary Potential + Air Mastery (2d8)

| Roll Result | Effect Achieved |
|-------------|----------------|
| 0 successes | Energy spent, no effect |
| 1 success | Reduced effect (one tier below energy investment) |
| 2 successes | Full effect as per energy investment |
| 3 successes | Enhanced effect (one tier above, capped at table maximum) |

### Overspending
Identical to external weaving rules. Spending beyond Mastery in any energy adds +1 to both dice on the roll per point over Mastery. Cannot spend beyond Potential absolute ceiling.

## Exhaustion Cost Framework

At the end of any scene or encounter where Complex Internal Manifestation was used:

| Situation | Fortitude Save Required |
|-----------|------------------------|
| Total energy spent within highest Mastery rank | No check |
| Total energy spent exceeded highest Mastery rank | Yes — 1 success required |
| Any single use overspent beyond Mastery | Yes — regardless of total |
| Any Presence effect used | Yes — regardless of total |

**Fortitude Save Results:**
- 2 successes: No Exhaustion gained
- 1 success: No Exhaustion gained, but cannot use Complex Internal Manifestation again until short rest
- 0 successes: Gain one Exhaustion level

## Expression Effects — Condition Reference

Fire Expression effects apply fire conditions from the stacking fire condition track. Conditions apply on the stated margin of success on the weapon attack roll.

| Margin | Condition Applied | Effect |
|--------|------------------|--------|
| Margin 1 | Singed (Level 1) | Add 1 to ability die on target's next action. Ends after their next action. |
| Margin 2 | Smoldering (Level 2) | Target takes 2 fire damage at the beginning of their next turn only. |
| Margin 3 | Ignited (Level 3) | Target takes 4 fire damage at the beginning of each turn. Requires 1 action to extinguish (Reflex save). |

Burning (Level 4) is not achievable through Expression effects. Expression adds fire to a strike — it does not engulf the target. Burning is external weave territory.

Existing fire condition stacking rules apply. A target already Smoldering who takes an Ignited-level hit upgrades to Ignited rather than stacking two Smoldering effects.

## Expression Effects — Damage and Life-Leach

**Bonus damage from Expression effects** uses energy damage rules: half DR applies.

| Primary Cost | Bonus Fire Damage | Condition Potential |
|-------------|------------------|-------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | +2 fire | Singed on margin 2+ |
| 5 (4+1) | +4 fire | Smoldering on margin 2+ |
| 7 (6+1) | +8 fire | Ignited on margin 2+ |

**Life-Leach variant** (available at Fire Mastery 4+, Negative Mastery 2+):
Returns flat HP per strike rather than a percentage of bonus damage. Limited to living targets only.

| Primary Cost | HP Returned per Strike |
|-------------|----------------------|
| 3 (2+1) | 1 HP |
| 5 (4+1) | 2 HP |
| 7 (6+1) | 4 HP |

---

## HP Cost Mechanic

Several Negative primary pairings offer an HP cost option. Paying 4 HP flat upgrades the effect one tier on the relevant table without spending additional energy. This represents deliberate self-sacrifice — burning the character's own vitality as fuel for the effect.

HP cost activations always trigger an Exhaustion check regardless of total energy spent. The cost is real and non-recoverable until rest.
