# D8 TTRPG - Part 3: Flaws

## FLAWS (Grant Points)

### Absent Mindedness (2)
Mind wanders frequently.
-   Initiative: -1
-   Tasks requiring concentration: Misfortune

### Addiction (3)
Substance has hold over you.
-   When deprived: Add 1 to all attribute die results

### Aversion (1)
Particular dislike for one thing. Will remove it from your presence.

### Bad Luck (6)
Luck is costly and inefficient.
-   Luck attribute functions normally for starting value and regeneration
-   Spending Luck costs 2 points per die reduction instead of 1
-   You still roll Luck for saves normally
-   Complications on partial success work normally

### Black Sheep (2)
Family has disowned you. No family support.

### Blind (7)
Lost sight in both eyes.
-   Permanently Blinded (see Blinded condition)

### Child (5)
Begin below minimum age, inexperienced.
-   Reduce all attributes by 1 (minimum 1)
-   Do no select a background.
-   +24 XP at creation to spend on advancement

### Code of Honor (3)
Rigid ethics code (8-10 tenets) you cannot break. GM approval required.

### Color Blindness (2)
Cannot distinguish colors.
-   Perception where color matters: Add 1 to die results

### Compulsive Behavior (2)
Irresistible activity (gambling, lying, etc.). Must make Will save to
resist.

### Cowardice (4)
Must make Will save when confronted with combat/fear or become Cowering.

### Cursed (10)
Affairs never go your way.
-   Luck starts at 1, can never exceed 1
-   Gain 2 Curse Points (work opposite: increase rolls)
-   Must use Curse before Luck
-   After using Luck, regain 2 Curse Points
-   Items carry curse for 1 week after leaving possession

### Dark Secret (1)
Something in past could ruin/humiliate you.

### Deafness (3)
Totally deaf.
-   Permanently Deafened (see Deafened condition)

### Deep Sleeper (1)
Difficult to awaken.
-   Require 2 successes to wake
-   First round after waking: Add 2 to all die results

### Delusion (2)
Believe something blatantly false. Cannot be convinced otherwise.

### Disfigured (5)
Missing appendage (hand, foot, arm, leg).
-   Missing hand/foot: Add 2 to related skill die results
-   Missing arm/leg: Related skills impossible or add 4 to die results

### Driving Goal (2)
Always-just-out-of-reach goal you constantly pursue.

### Duty Bound (2)
True to your word. Avoid lies, must keep promises.

### Enemy (1-5)
Someone/group has animosity toward you.
-   Minor (1): Occasional trouble
-   Powerful (3): Significant threat
-   Group/Extremely Powerful (5): Organized, constant danger

### Fanaticism (2)
Zealot about one belief. Take offense when insulted.

### Fealty (2)
Sworn allegiance to political faction. Must honor it.

### Greed (2)
Covet money above all. Will go to any length to gain more.

### Hard of Hearing (2)
-   Sound-based Perception: Add 1 to die results
-   Initiative: -1

### Hatred (3)
Overpowering contempt for someone/something. Hatred overrides other
emotions.

### Haunted (3)
Spirit only you see berates you.
-   Once per session, GM imposes Misfortune on a roll

### Hired Sword (3)
Mercenary reputation. Will do anything for price. No loyalty possible.

### Light Sensitive (2)
Not accustomed to bright light.
-   Permanently Dazzled in daylight (see Dazzled condition)
-   Normal at twilight/night with artificial light

### Mistaken Identity (1)
Common appearance, often mistaken for others.

### Mute (5)
Cannot vocalize.
-   Speech-based social skills automatically fail

### Nightmares (2)
Horrible nightmares prevent restful sleep.
-   Long rest only restores half Luck (rounded down)

### Orphan (1)
Forsaken as child, raised on streets/by others/in orphanage.

### Overconfidence (2)
Believe you're best at everything.
-   Must make Will saves to retreat from dangerous situations or admit
    you're wrong
-   NPCs may react negatively to your arrogance (GM discretion)

### Phobia (3)
Deathly afraid of something. Must make Will save or flee/cower when
confronted.

### Plague (7)
Spread disease to others.
-   Immune yourself
-   All within 10 feet must make daily Fortitude saves or contract disease

### Poor Memory (1)
Cannot remember faces, names, times, dates, places.
-   Cannot benefit from Eidetic Memory
-   History and Society: Add 2 to die results

### Poor Eyesight (2)
Myopia/hyperopia. Without corrective lenses:
-   Vision-based Perception: Add 2 to die results
-   Ranged Combat: Add 1 to die results

### Recognizable Trait (1)
Peculiar characteristic or marking makes you easily identifiable.

### Sickly (5)
Incurable debilitating illness.
-   Constitution: Permanently -2 (minimum 1)

### Speech Impediment (2)
Serious stutter/lisp/defect.
-   Persuasion, Perform, Deception when speaking: Add 2 to die results

### Touch of Evil (3)
Bloodline tainted with darkness.
-   Persuasion with good-aligned NPCs: Add 2 to die results
-   Intimidate: Subtract 2 from die results

### Unluckiness (3)
Nothing goes your way.
-   Once per session, GM forces reroll of successful roll, take worse
    result

### Vengeance (2)
Someone wronged you terribly. Life devoted to revenge.

### Weak Willed (3)
Hard time with mentally taxing/redundant tasks.
-   Will saves: Add 1 to die results
-   Cannot attempt same failed task more than 3 times
