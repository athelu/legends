# D8 TTRPG - Sample Bestiary (Revised)

## Using These Stat Blocks

These monsters follow the Bestiary Framework guidelines and demonstrate proper monster design for the D8 TTRPG system.

## Threat Rating 1/8

### Giant Rat
**Small beast**

- **Threat Rating:** 1/8
- **HP:** 12 (Con 2 × 6, Fragile)
- **DR:** 0
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Attributes:** Str 2 / Con 2 / Agi 4 / Dex 3 / Int 1 / Wis 3 / Cha 1 / Luck 2
- **Skills:** Melee Combat 2, Stealth 3, Perception 3
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft, Keen Smell
- **Initiative Bonus:** +4 (Agi 4)

**Attacks:**
- **Bite [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 4 + Melee Combat 2 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 5 ft
  - Margin 1: 3 damage (Small size, light natural weapon: 4 - 1 = 3)
  - Margin 2: 5 damage (3 + 2 Str)

**Special Abilities:**
- **Pack Tactics:** When an ally is within 5 feet of the target, subtract 1 from both attack dice
- **Keen Smell:** Scent-based Perception checks have Fortune
- **Disease Carrier:** On hit with margin 2, target must make Fortitude save (Con + Luck, 1 success needed) or contract Filth Fever
  - Incubation: 1d8 days
  - Effect: Poisoned condition until cured, Constitution checks add 1 to both dice
  - Cure: Two consecutive successful Fortitude saves (1/day) OR magical healing

**Creature Type Traits:**
- **Beast:** No special immunities

**Tactics:** Giant rats rely on Pack Tactics and numbers. They swarm lone targets, attempting to spread disease through repeated bites.

## Threat Rating 1/4

### Goblin
**Small humanoid, typically chaotic**

- **Threat Rating:** 1/4
- **HP:** 14 (Con 2 × 7, Standard)
- **DR:** 2 (leather armor + small shield, +1 DR from shield)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Attributes:** Str 2 / Con 2 / Agi 4 / Dex 3 / Int 2 / Wis 2 / Cha 2 / Luck 2
- **Skills:** Melee Combat 3, Ranged Combat 2, Stealth 4, Perception 2
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft
- **Equipment:** Scimitar (standard weapon), shortbow, leather armor, small shield
- **Initiative Bonus:** +4 (Agi 4)

**Attacks:**
- **Scimitar [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 4 + Melee Combat 3 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 5 ft
  - Margin 1: 5 damage (Small size, standard weapon: 6 - 1 = 5)
  - Margin 2: 7 damage (5 + 2 Str)
- **Shortbow [Combat]:** Ranged attack (Dex 3 + Ranged Combat 2)
  - Range: 80/320 ft
  - Short range (80 ft): 1 success = 5 damage, 2 successes = 8 damage (5 + 3 Dex)
  - Medium range (160 ft): 2 successes = 8 damage
  - Long range (320 ft): 2 successes needed, add 1 to both dice, 8 damage

**Reactions:**
- **Shield Block:** When hit by melee attack, choose one:
  - Force attacker to reroll one die (attacker chooses which)
  - Gain +2 DR against this attack only

**Special Abilities:**
- **Nimble Escape:** Can take Disengage or Hide as a [Minor] action instead of a standard action
- **Pack Tactics:** When an ally is within 5 feet of the target, subtract 1 from both attack dice

**Creature Type Traits:**
- **Humanoid:** No special immunities

**Tactics:** Goblins use hit-and-run tactics. They fire arrows from cover using superior numbers, then use Nimble Escape to retreat when enemies close. Shield-wielding goblins protect archers.

## Threat Rating 1/2

### Orc Warrior
**Medium humanoid, typically chaotic**

- **Threat Rating:** 1/2
- **HP:** 36 (Con 4 × 9, Tough)
- **DR:** 3 (hide armor + shield, +1 DR from shield)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Attributes:** Str 4 / Con 4 / Agi 3 / Dex 3 / Int 2 / Wis 3 / Cha 3 / Luck 2
- **Skills:** Melee Combat 4, Ranged Combat 2, Intimidate 3, Perception 2
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft
- **Equipment:** Greataxe OR battleaxe + shield, 2 javelins, hide armor
- **Initiative Bonus:** +3 (Agi 3)

**Attacks (Greataxe Configuration):**
- **Greataxe [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 3 + Melee Combat 4 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 5 ft
  - Margin 1: 10 damage (two-handed weapon)
  - Margin 2: 14 damage (10 + 4 Str)

**Attacks (Battleaxe + Shield Configuration):**
- **Battleaxe [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 3 + Melee Combat 4 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 5 ft
  - Margin 1: 8 damage (heavy one-handed weapon)
  - Margin 2: 12 damage (8 + 4 Str)

**Ranged Attack (Both Configurations):**
- **Javelin [Combat]:** Ranged attack (Dex 3 + Ranged Combat 2)
  - Range: 30/120 ft
  - Short range: 1 success = 6 damage, 2 successes = 9 damage (6 + 3 Dex)
  - Medium range: 2 successes = 9 damage

  - 1+ successes: Drop to 1 HP instead of 0 HP

**Creature Type Traits:**
- **Humanoid:** No special immunities

**Tactics:** Orcs charge aggressively using their Aggressive ability to close quickly. Greataxe wielders focus on devastating damage while shield bearers use Shield Block to protect themselves and hold the line. All orcs fight to the death using Relentless when dropped.

## Threat Rating 1

### Wolf Pack Leader
**Medium beast**

- **Threat Rating:** 1
- **HP:** 32 (Con 4 × 8, Standard)
- **DR:** 0
- **Speed:** 50 ft
- **Attributes:** Str 4 / Con 4 / Agi 5 / Dex 4 / Int 2 / Wis 4 / Cha 3 / Luck 3
- **Skills:** Melee Combat 5, Perception 5, Stealth 4, Survival 3
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft, Keen Hearing and Smell
- **Initiative Bonus:** +5 (Agi 5)

**Attacks:**
- **Bite [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 5 + Melee Combat 5 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 5 ft
  - Margin 1: 6 damage (standard natural weapon)
  - Margin 2: 10 damage (6 + 4 Str)
  - Special: On margin 2+, target must make Fortitude save (Con + Luck, 1 success) or be knocked Prone

**Special Abilities:**
- **Pack Tactics:** When an ally is within 5 feet of the target, subtract 1 from both attack dice

**Creature Type Traits:**
- **Beast:** No special immunities

**Tactics:** The pack leader coordinates wolf attacks, using Pack Tactics and Coordinated Strike to bring down large prey. It targets spellcasters and archers first, attempting to knock them prone for the pack to tear apart.

## Threat Rating 2

### Ghoul
**Medium undead**

- **Threat Rating:** 2
- **HP:** 52 (Con 4 × 13, Standard Undead)
- **DR:** 0
- **Speed:** 30 ft, Climb 30 ft
- **Attributes:** Str 3 / Con 4 / Agi 4 / Dex 3 / Int 2 / Wis 3 / Cha 2 / Luck 2
- **Skills:** Melee Combat 5, Stealth 4, Perception 3
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft
- **Initiative Bonus:** +4 (Agi 4)

**Attacks:**
- **Bite [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 4 + Melee Combat 5 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 5 ft
  - Margin 1: 6 damage (standard natural weapon)
  - Margin 2: 9 damage (6 + 3 Str)
- **Claws [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 4 + Melee Combat 5 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 5 ft
  - Margin 1: 4 damage (light natural weapon)
  - Margin 2: 7 damage (4 + 3 Str)
  - Special: On any hit (margin 1+), target must make Fortitude save (Con + Luck):
    - 0 successes: Paralyzed for 1 minute
    - 1+ successes: No effect
    - Paralyzed creatures can repeat save at end of each turn (2 successes ends early, 1 success adds Fortune to next save)

**Multiattack:**
- **Multiattack [Combat]:** The ghoul makes one bite and two claw attacks
  - Shared Attribute Roll (Agi 4): Roll once, apply to all three attacks
  - Roll Melee Combat separately for each attack (three rolls of 1d8, need <5 each)

**Special Abilities:**
- **Paralytic Claws:** Creatures hit by claw attacks must save vs paralysis (see Claws attack)
- **Spider Climb:** Can climb difficult surfaces including upside-down on ceilings without ability checks
- **Paralyze Immunity:** Ghouls and other undead are immune to ghoul paralysis

**Creature Type Traits:**
- **Undead:** Immune to poison damage, Poisoned condition, disease, exhaustion. Does not need air, food, drink, or sleep.

**Resistances/Immunities:**
- **Immunities:** Poison damage, Poisoned condition, disease, exhaustion

**Tactics:** Ghouls use stealth and climbing to ambush prey from unexpected angles. They use Multiattack to maximize chances of landing paralytic claw strikes, then feast on paralyzed victims. They gang up on dangerous foes to trigger multiple paralysis saves.

## Threat Rating 3

### Owlbear
**Large monstrosity**

- **Threat Rating:** 3
- **HP:** 108 (Con 6 × 18, Tough)
- **DR:** 2 (thick hide and feathers, natural armor)
- **Speed:** 40 ft
- **Attributes:** Str 6 / Con 6 / Agi 4 / Dex 3 / Int 1 / Wis 4 / Cha 2 / Luck 3
- **Skills:** Melee Combat 6, Perception 5
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft, Keen Sight and Smell
- **Initiative Bonus:** +4 (Agi 4)

**Attacks:**
- **Beak [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 4 + Melee Combat 6 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 10 ft (Large creature)
  - Margin 1: 10 damage (Large size, heavy natural weapon: 8 + 2 = 10)
  - Margin 2: 16 damage (10 + 6 Str)
- **Claws [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 4 + Melee Combat 6 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 10 ft (Large creature)
  - Margin 1: 10 damage (Large size, standard natural weapon: 8 + 2 = 10, representing both claws)
  - Margin 2: 16 damage (10 + 6 Str)

**Multiattack:**
- **Multiattack [Combat]:** The owlbear makes one beak attack and one claw attack
  - Shared Attribute Roll (Agi 4): Roll once, apply to both attacks
  - Roll Melee Combat separately for each attack (two rolls of 1d8, need <6 each)

**Special Abilities:**
- **Large Size:** Occupies 10×10 ft space, 10 ft reach
- **Keen Sight and Smell:** Vision and scent-based Perception checks have Fortune
- **Maul:** If both attacks (beak AND claw) hit the same target in one turn, target takes additional 8 damage (no attribute modifier, represents crushing embrace)
- **Aggressive Charge (Recharge 6-8):** If the owlbear moves at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits with a claw attack on the same turn, the target must make a Fortitude save (Con + Luck, 1 success) or be knocked Prone. If prone, owlbear can immediately make a beak attack as a [Free] action.

**Creature Type Traits:**
- **Monstrosity:** No special immunities

**Tactics:** Owlbears charge aggressively, attempting to trigger Aggressive Charge to knock prey prone. They use Multiattack every round, trying to trigger Maul for devastating damage. Wounded owlbears become frenzied and reckless.

## Threat Rating 3

### Basilisk
**Medium monstrosity**

- **Threat Rating:** 3
- **HP:** 90 (Con 5 × 18, Tough)
- **DR:** 4 (thick scales, natural armor)
- **Speed:** 20 ft
- **Attributes:** Str 5 / Con 5 / Agi 3 / Dex 2 / Int 1 / Wis 3 / Cha 2 / Luck 2
- **Skills:** Melee Combat 5, Stealth 4, Perception 3
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft
- **Initiative Bonus:** +3 (Agi 3)

**Attacks:**
- **Bite [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 3 + Melee Combat 5 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 5 ft
  - Margin 1: 8 damage (heavy natural weapon) + Poisoned condition (see below)
  - Margin 2: 13 damage (8 + 5 Str) + Poisoned condition

**Poison Bite:**
- On any hit, target becomes Poisoned (see Conditions):
  - All attribute dice: Add 1 to die results
  - Fortitude saves: Add 1 to die results
  - HP regeneration from rests reduced by half
  - Duration: 1 hour OR until cured by Medicine check (2 successes) or magical healing

**Special Abilities:**
- **Petrifying Gaze (Passive, at-will):** When a creature that can see the basilisk's eyes starts its turn within 30 feet, the basilisk can force it to make a Fortitude save (Con + Luck):
  - 0 successes: Begin turning to stone - Restrained condition, add 1 to all dice
  - At end of affected creature's next turn, make another Fortitude save:
    - 0 successes: Turned to stone (Petrified - effectively dead until restored by magic)
    - 1+ successes: Restrained condition ends, no longer turning to stone
  - **Averting Eyes:** Creatures can choose to avert eyes as a [Free] action. While averting:
    - Immune to petrifying gaze
    - All attacks against basilisk have Misfortune (roll 3d8, take worst 2)
    - Basilisk's attacks against averting creature subtract 1 from both dice
- **Thick Scales:** Natural DR 4 (already included in stat block)

**Creature Type Traits:**
- **Monstrosity:** No special immunities

**Resistances:**
- **Poison Resistance:** +1 DR against poison damage

**Tactics:** Basilisks rely on their petrifying gaze to control the battlefield. They wait for enemies to fail saves and become Restrained, then close for poisonous bites. Against multiple foes, they position to catch as many in their gaze as possible. Smart enemies avert their eyes but suffer severe combat penalties.

## Threat Rating 4

### Young Red Dragon
**Large dragon**

- **Threat Rating:** 4
- **HP:** 180 (Con 6 × 30, Massive)
- **DR:** 5 (dragon scales, natural armor)
- **Speed:** 40 ft, Climb 40 ft, Fly 80 ft
- **Attributes:** Str 6 / Con 6 / Agi 4 / Dex 4 / Int 4 / Wis 4 / Cha 5 / Luck 4
- **Skills:** Melee Combat 6, Perception 7, Stealth 4, Arcane 4, Intimidate 6
- **Senses:** Darkvision 120 ft, Blindsight 30 ft
- **Initiative Bonus:** +4 (Agi 4)

**Attacks:**
- **Bite [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 4 + Melee Combat 6 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 10 ft (Large creature with long neck)
  - Margin 1: 12 damage (Large, two-handed equivalent) + 6 fire damage
  - Margin 2: 18 damage (12 + 6 Str) + 6 fire damage
- **Claw [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 4 + Melee Combat 6 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 5 ft
  - Margin 1: 8 damage (Large, light weapon: 6 + 2)
  - Margin 2: 14 damage (8 + 6 Str)

**Multiattack:**
- **Multiattack [Combat]:** The dragon makes one bite attack and two claw attacks
  - Shared Attribute Roll (Agi 4): Roll once, apply to all three attacks
  - Roll Melee Combat separately for each attack (three rolls of 1d8, need <6 each)

**Breath Weapon:**
- **Fire Breath (Recharge 5-8) [Combat]:** 30-foot cone. Each creature in area makes Reflex save (Agi + Luck):
  - Add 1 to both Reflex save dice (represents difficulty of avoiding)
  - 0 successes: 40 fire damage
  - 1 success: 20 fire damage
  - 2+ successes: 10 fire damage

**Special Abilities:**
- **Large Size:** Occupies 10×10 ft space, reach varies by attack
- **Frightful Presence (3/day):** Each creature of dragon's choice within 60 feet that can see it must make Will save (Wis + Luck, 1 success needed):
  - Failure: Frightened for 1 minute (can repeat save at end of each turn)
  - Success: Immune to this dragon's Frightful Presence for 24 hours
- **Dragon Senses:** Darkvision 120 ft, Blindsight 30 ft (perceive surroundings without sight within 30 ft)

**Creature Type Traits:**
- **Dragon:** Darkvision 120 ft, Blindsight 30 ft, can inspire fear

**Resistances/Immunities:**
- **Immunity:** Fire damage, Frightened condition (dragons are immune to fear)

**Resilient Defense:** 2/day - When fails a saving throw, can choose to succeed instead

**Tactics:** Young dragons open with Frightful Presence to scatter weaker foes, then unleash Fire Breath on clustered enemies. They use flight to stay mobile, strafing with breath weapon when recharged. In melee, they use Multiattack to focus down dangerous spellcasters. Dragons retreat if reduced below 60 HP, remembering enemies for future vengeance.

## Threat Rating 5

### Stone Golem
**Large construct**

- **Threat Rating:** 5
- **HP:** 180 (Con 6 × 30, Massive)
- **DR:** 6 (stone body, natural armor)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Attributes:** Str 7 / Con 6 / Agi 2 / Dex 2 / Int 1 / Wis 3 / Cha 1 / Luck 2
- **Skills:** Melee Combat 5
- **Senses:** Darkvision 120 ft, Blindsight 60 ft
- **Initiative Bonus:** +2 (Agi 2)

**Attacks:**
- **Slam [Combat]:** Melee attack (Agi 2 + Melee Combat 7 vs opponent's defense)
  - Reach: 10 ft (Large creature)
  - Margin 1: 12 damage (Large, two-handed natural: 10 + 2)
  - Margin 2: 19 damage (12 + 7 Str)

**Multiattack:**
- **Multiattack [Combat]:** The golem makes two slam attacks
  - Shared Attribute Roll (Agi 2): Roll once, apply to both attacks
  - Roll Melee Combat separately for each attack (two rolls of 1d8, need <5 each)

**Special Abilities:**
- **Large Size:** Occupies 10×10 ft space, 10 ft reach
- **Magic Resistance:** Subtract 1 from both dice on saving throws against magical effects
- **Slow (Recharge 6-8) [Combat]:** Targets up to 3 creatures within 10 feet. Targets make Will save (Wis + Luck):
  - 0 successes: Can only take 2 actions per turn (instead of 3) and cannot take reactions for 1 minute
  - 1+ successes: No effect
  - Add 1 to both Will save dice (difficulty of resisting)
  - Affected creatures can repeat save at end of each turn (1 success ends early)
- **Immutable Form:** Immune to any weave that would alter its form (transformation, Beastial Transformation, etc.)

**Creature Type Traits:**
- **Construct:** Immune to poison damage, Poisoned condition, disease, exhaustion, Paralyzed, Petrified. Mindless: immune to mind-affecting effects (Beguiling Weave, fear, Sleep), fear, psychic effects. Does not need air, food, drink, or sleep.

**Resistances/Immunities:**
- **Immunities:** Poison damage, Poisoned condition, disease, exhaustion, Paralyzed, Petrified, charm, fear, psychic damage, non-magical piercing and slashing damage
- **Magic Resistance:** Subtract 1 from both dice on saves vs magical effects

**Tactics:** Stone golems advance inexorably, using Slow to cripple multiple enemies before crushing them with massive slams. They ignore most damage types and magic, making them terrifying juggernauts. Golems follow orders literally and never retreat.
